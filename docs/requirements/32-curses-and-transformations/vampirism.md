# Vampirism

As a player, I want to become a vampire with powerful night abilities but crippling daylight weakness, so that the curse offers a dramatic risk-reward transformation path.

## Details

- Contracted by: vampire lord bite (rare encounter), drinking ancient blood, dark ritual quest
- Vampire abilities:
  - **Blood Drain**: melee attack that heals for 100% of damage dealt
  - **Mesmerize**: charm any humanoid for 5 turns (Charisma-enhanced)
  - **Bat Form**: fly over terrain, very fast, but fragile
  - **Shadow Meld**: become invisible in darkness indefinitely
  - **Undying**: if killed, reform at your coffin after 50 turns (must place a coffin somewhere)
- Weaknesses:
  - **Sunlight**: take 5 damage per turn in daylight, stats halved
  - **Holy**: holy magic deals 3x damage, cannot enter temples
  - **Running Water**: cannot cross rivers or enter underwater areas
  - **Garlic**: reduces all stats by 3 when adjacent to garlic items
  - **No Reflection**: certain mirror-based puzzles become unsolvable
- Must feed on blood regularly: drain sleeping NPCs, blood potions, or enemy corpses
- Starvation: not feeding for 200 turns causes frenzy (attack anything nearby)
- Cure: extremely difficult, requires a legendary quest and rare ingredients
- Vampire lord transformation at high vampire level: fly permanently, command lesser vampires, nearly immortal

- [ ] Vampirism is contractable and grants all abilities
- [ ] Daylight and holy weaknesses apply correctly
- [ ] Blood feeding mechanic and starvation work

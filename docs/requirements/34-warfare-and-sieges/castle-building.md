# Castle Building

As a player, I want to build and upgrade a personal castle that serves as a home base and can be defended during sieges, so that territory control has a tangible physical dimension.

## Details

- **Castle Location**: purchase land in any region (price varies by location); build on a claimed plot
- **Construction Phases**:
  - Phase 1: wooden palisade, small keep (bedroom, storage, basic crafting); cost: 500 gold, 50 wood
  - Phase 2: stone walls, guard tower, armory, stables; cost: 2000 gold, 200 stone, 50 iron
  - Phase 3: full castle with moat, drawbridge, multiple towers, great hall, dungeon, enchanting room; cost: 10000 gold, 500 stone, 200 iron, rare materials
  - Phase 4: legendary fortress with magical wards, teleportation circle, siege weapons, automated defenses; cost: 50000 gold + quest materials
- **Castle Features** (unlocked by phase):
  - Bedroom: free resting, safe storage, dream control (see lucid dreaming)
  - Armory: equipment storage, repair station
  - Trophy Room: display killed boss heads, legendary items, quest mementos
  - Library: store collected books, research spells, identify items
  - Training Yard: practice combat, train companions, spar with NPCs
  - Dungeon: imprison captured enemies, interrogate for information
  - Throne Room: receive NPC visitors, hold court, manage territory
- **Staff**: hire NPCs to maintain the castle (cook, blacksmith, guards, healer); each provides passive bonuses
- **Castle Defense**: during siege events, the player must defend walls, repair breaches, command guard positions, operate siege weapons
- **Upgrade Specialization**: fortify for defense (extra walls, murder holes) OR optimize for crafting (extra workshops, bigger library) OR enhance magic (ley line tap, ritual chamber)
- Castle appears on the world map as a unique tile; other NPCs may visit or attack it

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All construction phases build correctly with required resources
- [ ] Castle features unlock and function per phase
- [ ] Staff NPCs provide listed passive bonuses
- [ ] Castle defense gameplay works during sieges
- [ ] Specialization choices alter castle capabilities

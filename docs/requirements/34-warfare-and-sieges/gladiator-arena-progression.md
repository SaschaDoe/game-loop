# Gladiator Arena Progression

As a player, I want to fight through a tiered gladiator arena with escalating challenges and fame, so that combat has a structured competitive progression with unique rewards.

## Details

- Arena located in the largest city; accessible once discovered
- Tiers: Bronze (Lv1-10), Silver (Lv11-20), Gold (Lv21-35), Platinum (Lv36-50), Champion
- Each tier has 5 fights with increasing difficulty:
  - Round 1: single common enemy
  - Round 2: pair of enemies
  - Round 3: enemy with environmental hazard (lava floor, shrinking arena)
  - Round 4: elite enemy with special mechanics
  - Round 5: tier boss (named gladiator champion with unique weapon and fighting style)
- Between fights: rest, buy supplies from arena vendors, talk to other gladiators (LLM dialogue)
- Fame system: winning fights increases fame; famous gladiators are recognized in towns, get discounts, and attract groupies
- Sponsor system: wealthy NPCs offer gold bonuses for winning with style (use specific abilities, no damage taken, speed kills)
- Rewards per tier: unique arena armor set pieces, arena-exclusive weapons, title upgrades
- Champion tier: fight the Grand Champion — victory grants a legendary weapon and the title "Grand Champion"
- Optional: bet on NPC gladiator fights (gambling system)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All arena tiers and fights are implemented
- [ ] Fame increases with victories and has world effects
- [ ] Sponsor challenges provide bonus rewards
- [ ] Grand Champion fight is a memorable boss encounter

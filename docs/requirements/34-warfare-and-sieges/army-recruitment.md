# Army Recruitment

As a player, I want to recruit and maintain a personal army for large-scale battles, so that I can build military power and lead forces in faction conflicts.

## Details

- Recruit soldiers from: taverns (mercenaries), faction barracks (loyal troops), liberated prisoners (grateful fighters)
- Unit types: footmen (balanced), archers (ranged), cavalry (fast, charge bonus), mages (AoE spells), healers (support)
- Army size limited by: Charisma stat, faction rank, gold for upkeep
- Upkeep: soldiers require gold per 100 turns; unpaid soldiers desert
- Training: invest gold and time to upgrade units (recruit -> veteran -> elite)
- Army morale: affected by victories, defeats, pay, food supply, and player reputation
- Low morale: soldiers refuse orders, desert, or switch sides
- Army camp: set up on the overworld map for rest, healing, and recruitment
- Named lieutenants: elite NPCs who lead sub-units and have their own personalities and questlines

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Soldiers are recruitable from multiple sources
- [ ] Army upkeep costs are deducted automatically
- [ ] Unit training upgrades stats
- [ ] Morale system affects army behavior

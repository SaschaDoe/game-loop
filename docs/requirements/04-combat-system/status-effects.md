# Status Effects

As a player, I want attacks and spells to inflict status effects on enemies (and vice versa), so that combat has more depth beyond raw damage numbers.

## Details

- Poison: deals damage over time each turn
- Burn: deals fire damage over time, reduces armor
- Freeze: slows target (acts every other turn)
- Blind: reduces accuracy significantly
- Stun: target loses next turn entirely
- Curse: reduces all stats temporarily
- Regeneration: heals HP each turn (positive buff)
- Status effects have durations (number of turns)
- Resistance stats reduce chance of being afflicted
- Visual indicators on affected entities (color change or symbol)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All status effects apply and tick correctly
- [ ] Durations count down and effects expire
- [ ] Resistance reduces application chance
- [ ] Visual indicators display on affected entities

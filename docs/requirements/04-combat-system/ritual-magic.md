# Ritual Magic

As a player, I want to perform powerful ritual spells that require multiple turns of casting and special components, so that preparation and planning enable effects far beyond normal spells.

## Details

- Rituals: multi-turn spells (3-10 turns of channeling) that produce massive effects
- Ritual requirements: specific components consumed on cast, uninterrupted channeling, sometimes a specific location
- Ritual spells:
  - **Ward of Protection** (3 turns, salt + candles): create a 5x5 safe zone that repels all enemies for 50 turns
  - **Summoning Circle** (5 turns, chalk + blood + soul gem): summon a powerful demon or celestial ally for 30 turns
  - **Resurrection** (4 turns, phoenix feather + diamond + holy water): revive any dead NPC or companion at full HP
  - **World Scrying** (3 turns, crystal ball + mana crystal): reveal the entire map for 100 turns, including all enemies and secrets
  - **Curse Removal** (5 turns, holy water + wolfsbane + moonstone): remove any curse, disease, or transformation
  - **Weather Control** (4 turns, storm crystal + wind essence): change the weather for 200 turns (clear skies, storm, fog, etc.)
  - **Mass Teleport** (6 turns, teleport stone x5): teleport self, all companions, and all nearby allies to any visited location
  - **Armageddon** (10 turns, void crystal + dragon blood + ancient rune): deal 100 damage to every creature on the current map (including allies). Forbidden.
- Interruption: taking damage during channeling has 75% chance to break the ritual, wasting components
- Ritual sites: ancient circles boost ritual power and reduce cast time by 1 turn
- Only learnable from ancient tomes, hermit mages, or faction questlines

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All rituals require correct components and turns
- [ ] Channeling can be interrupted by damage
- [ ] Ritual effects are dramatically more powerful than normal spells
- [ ] Ritual sites provide cast-time reduction

# Festivals and Holidays

As a player, I want towns to hold festivals during certain seasons with unique activities and rewards, so that visiting towns at the right time offers special experiences.

## Details

- Spring: Planting Festival — help farmers for seed rewards, dance minigame, flower crown cosmetic
- Summer: Tournament of Blades — arena fights with NPCs, winner gets a unique weapon
- Autumn: Harvest Feast — cooking contest, pumpkin carving (decorations for housing), bonus food items
- Winter: Night of Lanterns — ghost stories by LLM NPCs, gift exchange with companions, rare winter-only shop
- Festivals last 50 turns in each town
- Festival NPCs have special dialogue and moods (festive, celebratory)
- Unique festival quests only available during the event
- Missing a festival means waiting until next year (season cycle)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Festivals trigger during correct seasons
- [ ] Festival activities and minigames are functional
- [ ] Festival-exclusive rewards are obtainable
- [ ] Festival quests are available during the event only

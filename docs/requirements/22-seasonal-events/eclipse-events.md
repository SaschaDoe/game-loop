# Eclipse Events

As a player, I want rare solar and lunar eclipses to trigger dramatic world changes and unique encounters, so that celestial events create memorable moments.

## Details

- **Eclipse Frequency**: solar eclipse every 100 in-game days, lunar eclipse every 50 in-game days (announced 5 days ahead by astronomers/NPCs)
- **Solar Eclipse Effects** (lasts 10 in-game hours):
  - World goes dark at midday; nocturnal creatures spawn during daytime
  - Shadow creatures emerge from a temporary Shadow Rift (unique dungeon entrance, disappears after eclipse)
  - Solar-powered items and abilities are disabled; moon-powered ones are boosted
  - Plants wilt temporarily; herbalism gathering fails during eclipse
  - NPCs panic or hold eclipse rituals; some become hostile (eclipse madness)
- **Lunar Eclipse Effects** (lasts one full night):
  - Moon turns red; lycanthropes are forced to transform and become feral
  - Undead are 50% stronger; holy damage is 50% weaker
  - The Veil between worlds thins: ghosts become visible, dimensional portals flicker open
  - Rare lunar herbs bloom (only harvestable during lunar eclipse)
  - Tides shift: coastal areas flood, revealing underwater caves
- **Eclipse Boss**: during each eclipse type, a unique boss spawns that can only be fought during that event
  - Solar: The Umbral Colossus (shadow titan)
  - Lunar: The Blood Moon Hydra (regenerates heads under red moonlight)
- **Eclipse Prophecies**: NPCs and lore books hint at what each eclipse will bring

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Eclipses occur on schedule and are announced in advance
- [ ] Solar eclipse darkens the world and spawns shadow creatures
- [ ] Lunar eclipse triggers lycanthrope transformations and undead buffs
- [ ] Eclipse-exclusive bosses spawn and are defeatable
- [ ] Rare eclipse resources are only available during the event

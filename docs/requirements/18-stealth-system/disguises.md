# Disguises

As a player, I want to wear disguises to pass as faction members or specific NPCs, so that I can infiltrate restricted areas and deceive enemies.

## Details

- Equip faction uniforms to be treated as a member by NPCs of that faction
- Disguise effectiveness based on Charisma and the NPC's familiarity with their own members
- Close interaction with faction NPCs risks being exposed (Charisma check)
- Certain NPCs can see through any disguise (faction leaders, guards with special training)
- Disguises acquired by: looting from enemies, buying from black market, quest rewards
- Performing hostile actions while disguised immediately breaks the disguise
- Monster disguises: wear a monster skin to reduce aggro from that monster type

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Disguises change NPC faction reactions
- [ ] Exposure checks trigger on close interaction
- [ ] Hostile actions break the disguise
- [ ] Monster-type disguises reduce aggro

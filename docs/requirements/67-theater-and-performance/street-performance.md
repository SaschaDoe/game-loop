# Street Performance

As a player, I want to perform on streets and in public squares as a busker earning tips and attracting attention, so that performance is accessible anywhere without needing a theater.

## Details

- **Performance Spots**: any public area in a town; busy intersections and market squares attract more audience; some spots are "claimed" by NPC buskers (negotiate, compete, or find another spot)
- **Performance Types**:
  - **Juggling**: DEX-based; juggle items from inventory (daggers more impressive than apples); drop chance decreases with skill; adding more items = more tips but higher drop risk
  - **Fire Breathing**: dangerous but lucrative; CON save or take burn damage; successful performance draws large crowds; uses alchemical fire oil (consumable)
  - **Acrobatics**: DEX + Athletics; flips, handstands, tightrope walking; increasing difficulty combos; crowd gasps at near-misses
  - **Magic Tricks**: sleight of hand illusions (not real magic); Deception + DEX; card tricks, disappearing coins, cups and balls; Perception-high NPCs may expose fakes
  - **Puppet Show**: CHA + Performance; use crafted puppets to tell stories; children love it (+reputation with families); can satirize local politicians (risky but popular)
  - **Fortune Telling**: CHA + Insight (or actual divination magic); read palms, cards, tea leaves; if using real magic, predictions come true; if faking, Deception check; some NPCs become regular clients
- **Crowd Building**: performance starts with 0-3 watchers; each successful turn attracts 1-2 more; crowd caps based on location and time of day; larger crowd = more tips per turn
- **Hazards**: pickpockets target the crowd (player loses reputation if audience is robbed); city guards may demand a busking permit (10 gold); rain disperses the crowd; rival busker challenges (performance duel)
- **Tips**: gold accumulates per turn based on crowd size x performance quality; occasional bonus tips (wealthy NPC tosses a gem, a merchant offers a trade deal, an NPC offers a quest)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All performance types use correct skill checks
- [ ] Crowd size grows based on performance success
- [ ] Hazards trigger at appropriate frequencies
- [ ] Tips calculate correctly from crowd size and quality
- [ ] Busking permits and rival buskers create gameplay friction

# Lost Civilization Ruins

As a player, I want to explore ruins of an advanced ancient civilization with technology beyond current understanding, so that dungeon exploration includes sci-fi crossover elements.

## Details

- **Ancient Civilization**: the "Precursors" — a race that vanished millennia ago; their technology blends magic and machinery
- **Ruin Types**:
  - **Research Facility**: laboratories with preserved experiments (containment failures = escaped monsters), data crystals (lore), prototype weapons
  - **Power Station**: generates energy from ley lines; reactivating it powers ancient devices across the world; overloading it causes a magical EMP
  - **Archive**: vast library of crystallized knowledge; each crystal teaches a skill, spell, or language; some are corrupted (sanity damage or false information)
  - **Factory**: automated assembly lines still producing constructs; reprogrammable if the player has Artificer skill; otherwise hostile
  - **Observatory**: tracks celestial events; reveals eclipse schedules, dimensional rifts, and star map (hidden dungeon locations)
- **Ancient Technology**:
  - Holographic displays (ASCII art projections showing historical scenes)
  - Gravity lifts (vertical fast travel within ruins)
  - Force fields (impassable barriers requiring specific keycards or puzzle solutions)
  - Auto-turrets (ranged enemies that can be hacked with Artificer skill to become allies)
  - Stasis pods (sometimes contain preserved Precursor NPCs — can be awakened)
- **Precursor Items**: energy blades (lightsaber-equivalent, highest melee damage), personal shields (absorb first hit per combat), data tablets (quest items), power cells (universal crafting component)
- **Ruin Hazards**: radiation zones (HP drain without protection), unstable reactors (timed escape sequences), malfunctioning AI (tricks players with false information)
- **Awakened Precursor**: extremely rare; an NPC from the ancient civilization; speaks an unknown language initially; teaches lost technologies and reveals world history

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All ruin types generate with unique layouts and encounters
- [ ] Ancient technology items function with correct effects
- [ ] Precursor items are significantly powerful but rare
- [ ] Ruin hazards create meaningful danger
- [ ] Awakened Precursor NPC dialogue works via LLM

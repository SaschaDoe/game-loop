# Escort Missions

As a player, I want to escort NPCs through dangerous areas, so that I can earn rewards while protecting vulnerable characters and building relationships.

## Details

- NPC asks for protection while traveling from point A to point B
- The escorted NPC follows the player and has limited combat ability
- Ambushes and encounters are scripted along the escort route
- If the NPC dies, the quest fails (can be retried)
- Escorted NPC has dialogue during the journey (LLM-powered small talk, backstory)
- Successful escort builds strong relationship with the NPC
- Some escort NPCs offer to join as companions afterwards

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Escorted NPC follows the player reliably
- [ ] Ambush encounters trigger along the route
- [ ] NPC death causes quest failure
- [ ] Journey dialogue is contextual and engaging

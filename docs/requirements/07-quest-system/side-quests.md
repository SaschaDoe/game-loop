# Side Quests

As a player, I want optional side quests offered by NPCs, so that I have additional content to explore and earn rewards outside the main storyline.

## Details

- Side quests are offered by NPCs based on their problems, profession, or faction
- Types: investigation, delivery, collection, protection, revenge, romance
- Some side quests are procedurally generated; others are hand-crafted
- Side quests can have multiple solution paths (combat, stealth, diplomacy)
- Completing side quests rewards XP, gold, items, and relationship boosts
- Some side quests unlock after reaching certain story milestones or relationship levels
- Side quests can have consequences that affect the broader world

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Side quests are discoverable through NPC interaction
- [ ] Multiple solution paths exist for key side quests
- [ ] Rewards are granted on completion
- [ ] World state changes reflect quest outcomes

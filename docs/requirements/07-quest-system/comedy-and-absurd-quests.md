# Comedy and Absurd Quests

As a player, I want to encounter humorous and absurd quests that break the tension, so that the game has tonal variety and moments of genuine laughter.

## Details

- Comedy quest examples:
  - **The Chicken Crusade**: a farmer's chickens have escaped into a dungeon; retrieve all 7 (each in increasingly dangerous rooms)
  - **The Bard's Worst Song**: a bard wrote a song so bad it literally curses anyone who hears it; find and destroy all copies
  - **Identity Crisis**: an NPC is convinced they're the player character and mimics everything you do
  - **The Mimic Infestation**: everything in a dungeon is a mimic — doors, chests, floors, even the stairs
  - **Tax Collector from Hell**: a bureaucratic demon demands you fill out paperwork to proceed (actual form-filling puzzle)
  - **The Pacifist Dragon**: a dragon that just wants to bake cookies and needs rare ingredients
- Comedy NPCs have exaggerated personalities (LLM prompted for comedic tone)
- Fourth-wall breaks: rare moments where NPCs reference game mechanics ("Why do you keep opening every barrel?")
- Comedic rewards: silly cosmetics, joke weapons that are surprisingly effective, title "Chicken Wrangler"

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Comedy quests are completable with humorous outcomes
- [ ] LLM dialogue supports comedic tone for relevant NPCs
- [ ] Comedic rewards are functional and equippable
- [ ] Fourth-wall moments are rare and well-placed

# Bounty Hunting

As a player, I want to take on bounty contracts to hunt specific targets, so that I have repeatable combat-focused content with clear objectives and rewards.

## Details

- Bounty board in towns lists available contracts
- Targets: specific named monsters, bandit leaders, escaped criminals
- Each bounty has a description, last known location, difficulty rating, and reward
- Bounties require finding and killing or capturing the target
- Capturing alive pays more but is harder (requires non-lethal approach)
- Bounty targets are tougher than regular enemies with unique abilities
- Completing bounties increases reputation with the bounty guild

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Bounty board displays available contracts
- [ ] Targets spawn at their described locations
- [ ] Kill and capture options both work
- [ ] Rewards and reputation are granted on completion

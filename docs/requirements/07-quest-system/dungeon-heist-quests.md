# Dungeon Heist Quests

As a player, I want heist-style quests where I must plan and execute a break-in to steal a specific item, so that stealth and planning are rewarded alongside combat.

## Details

- Heist quests: infiltrate a guarded location to steal a target item and escape
- Planning phase: gather intel on guard patrols, find blueprints, recruit specialists
- Multiple approaches: stealth (avoid all guards), disguise (walk in as someone else), brute force (fight through), tunneling (find or dig an alternate entrance)
- Timed escape: after grabbing the target, alarm triggers and you must reach the exit
- Guard AI: patrol routes, alert phases, reinforcement calls
- Specialist NPCs: a lockpicker, a distraction artist, a getaway driver (each recruited through side quests)
- Loot bonus: steal extra valuables while inside for bonus gold
- Failed heist: arrested (lose items, pay bail) or blacklisted by the faction

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Planning phase provides useful intel
- [ ] Multiple approaches are viable to completion
- [ ] Timed escape creates tension after alarm
- [ ] Failure has meaningful consequences

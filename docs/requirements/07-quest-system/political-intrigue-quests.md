# Political Intrigue Quests

As a player, I want quests involving court politics, betrayal, and power struggles, so that social and diplomatic gameplay offers complex challenges.

## Details

- Noble courts with competing houses vying for influence
- Quests: forge alliances, sabotage rivals, gather blackmail, arrange marriages, prevent assassinations
- Information is currency: learn secrets through dialogue, eavesdropping, and stolen documents
- Betrayal mechanic: NPCs you trust may double-cross you based on their hidden agendas
- Player can become a noble advisor, spymaster, or power behind the throne
- Court events: balls, councils, trials — social encounters with multiple NPCs
- Reputation shifts based on which house you support
- LLM dialogue enables nuanced political negotiations

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Court NPCs have competing agendas and alliances
- [ ] Information gathering and blackmail mechanics work
- [ ] Betrayal events trigger based on NPC hidden goals
- [ ] Political quests affect faction and town reputation

# Romance Questlines

As a player, I want optional romance storylines with certain NPCs, so that my character can form deep personal bonds that add emotional weight to the adventure.

## Details

- 4-6 romanceable NPCs across different towns and factions
- Romance progression: acquaintance -> friendship -> attraction -> courtship -> committed
- Progression requires: high relationship score, completing personal quests, gift-giving, meaningful dialogue choices
- LLM-powered romantic dialogue: flirting, deep conversations about fears and dreams, love letters
- Each romance has a unique questline arc (e.g., saving their village, curing their curse, earning their family's approval)
- Romance affects gameplay: partner gives unique gifts, warns of dangers, provides a safe-house
- Jealousy: pursuing multiple romances simultaneously causes conflict
- Breakups: betrayal or neglect can end the romance with emotional consequences
- Tasteful and story-driven; focused on emotional connection and character development
- All romances are available regardless of player character gender

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Romance progression stages work correctly
- [ ] LLM dialogue supports romantic interactions
- [ ] Each romance has a unique quest arc
- [ ] Jealousy and breakup mechanics function

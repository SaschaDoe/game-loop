# Potion Brewing

As a player, I want to brew custom potions by combining ingredients at an alchemy table, so that I can create powerful and unique consumables tailored to my needs.

## Details

- Alchemy table required (found in towns, player housing, or portable alchemy kit item)
- Recipe format: base liquid + 2-3 reagents + optional catalyst
- Base liquids: water (neutral), alcohol (enhances duration), oil (makes throwable), blood (dark magic)
- Reagent effects stack: healing herb + healing herb = stronger healing potion
- Mixing incompatible reagents creates volatile potions (explosion on use, or random effect)
- Catalyst gems amplify effects: ruby (fire), sapphire (ice), emerald (nature), diamond (all)
- Potion colors are randomized per playthrough; must identify or experiment to learn effects
- Discovery journal: once brewed, a recipe is permanently recorded
- Rare recipes: potions of invisibility, flying, giant strength, polymorph, time slow

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Ingredient combination produces correct potions
- [ ] Incompatible ingredients create volatile results
- [ ] Catalysts amplify potion effects
- [ ] Recipe journal permanently records discoveries

# Academy Quests

Quests make the Academy feel alive. They overlap with lessons, build relationships, and reveal history. Each quest has objectives, rewards, and consequences.

---

## Main Quest Chain

These quests trigger automatically as the school year progresses. Completing them is optional but enriches the experience.

### US-AA-48: "First Day Jitters"
**As a** new student, **I explore** the campus on Day 1 and introduce myself to each professor, **so that** I learn the layout and meet the cast.

**Objectives:**
1. Visit the Library and talk to Librarian Maren
2. Visit the Alchemy Tower and talk to Professor Thornwick
3. Visit the Enchantment Lab and talk to Professor Ashveil (she's dismissive until you prove yourself)
4. Visit the Practice Arena and examine the dungeon entrance
5. Talk to Rod in the dormitory (he's stuck trying to unpack — his trunk is too heavy)

**Rewards:** +25 XP, campus map marked in journal, Rod friendship +1

**Trigger:** Automatically suggested after enrollment dialogue. Quest appears in journal.

### US-AA-49: "Roderick's Disaster"
**As Rod's friend**, **I help** him clean up an alchemy explosion before Professor Thornwick's inspection, **so that** our friendship deepens and I learn about the alchemy tower.

**Trigger:** Day 5 (after Lesson 2). Rod runs to you in a panic.

**Objectives:**
1. Go to the Alchemy Tower
2. Clean up 3 puddles of volatile residue (interact with tiles — they damage you if you step on them without cleaning)
3. Repair the cracked cauldron (requires 1 Moonwater Vial — Thornwick's supply closet has one, but taking it is technically stealing)
4. Complete before Thornwick returns (timer: 50 turns)

**Rewards:** +30 XP, Rod friendship +2, if you used the Moonwater Vial: Thornwick later notices and you must explain (honesty = he forgives; lie = he catches you later with a minor consequence)

**Failure:** If timer expires, Thornwick discovers the mess. Rod gets detention. He doesn't blame you.

### US-AA-50: "Library Card"
**As a** student, **I retrieve** an overdue book from the practice dungeon for Librarian Maren, **so that** I earn library privileges and explore the dungeon.

**Trigger:** Day 3 (Maren mentions a student took a book into the dungeon and never returned it)

**Objectives:**
1. Descend into the practice dungeon
2. Find the book on dungeon level 1 or 2 (spawns as a loot item on a specific tile)
3. Return the book to Maren

**Rewards:** +40 XP, library access expanded (Maren lets you browse the restricted section entrance), 1 random reagent, Maren teaches a minor divination trick (+1 sight radius for that dungeon visit)

**Note:** The book is titled "A Brief History of the Academy" — reading it before returning reveals founding lore

### US-AA-51: "The Study Group"
**As Elara's potential friend**, **I find** a rare reagent she needs from the dungeon, **so that** she begins to trust me.

**Trigger:** Day 10 (Elara approaches the player — her first initiation of contact). Requires at least 1 prior conversation.

**Objectives:**
1. Elara needs a Dreamleaf specimen from the deep practice dungeon (level 3+)
2. Descend and find the Dreamleaf
3. Return to Elara

**Rewards:** +35 XP, Elara friendship +3, Elara teaches the player an alchemy tip: "If you add Starfern to almost any mixture, it stabilizes. The professors won't teach you that."

**Consequence:** This is the turning point — Elara begins trusting the player with deeper conversations

### US-AA-52: "Blackmere's Challenge"
**As Dorian's rival**, **I am challenged** to a spell duel in the arena, **so that** the rivalry reaches a climax.

**Trigger:** Day 14 (after Lesson 4). Dorian challenges you publicly in the courtyard.

**Objectives:**
1. Meet Dorian in the Practice Arena
2. Fight Dorian in a structured duel:
   - Dorian has 15 HP, 3 ATK, casts Firebolt and Arcane Ward
   - 3-round structure: each round, both cast a spell + melee
   - If player wins: Dorian grudgingly respects you
   - If player loses: Dorian gloats but the rivalry continues
   - If player refuses to fight: Dorian mocks you, but Fenn says "Smart. He practiced for a week."

**Rewards:**
- Win: +50 XP, "Duelist" title, Dorian's respect (affects later dialogue)
- Lose: +20 XP (participation), Dorian's contempt (temporary — he forgets by exam time)
- Refuse: No XP, but Fenn friendship +1 ("Finally, someone sensible")

**Note:** Dorian cheats subtly — he pre-casts Arcane Ward before the duel starts. Observant players (True Sight or high perception) can notice.

### US-AA-53: "Missing Ingredient"
**As a** student, **I investigate** a theft from the Alchemy Tower, **so that** I uncover school intrigue and develop detective skills.

**Trigger:** Day 17. Thornwick is distressed — a rare Phoenix Ash sample is missing.

**Objectives:**
1. Talk to Thornwick for details (the sample was in a locked cabinet; no signs of forced entry)
2. Investigate the Alchemy Tower (examine the cabinet, find a trace of Arcane Dust)
3. Talk to suspects:
   - Rod? ("I can barely open a door without breaking it. A locked cabinet?")
   - Elara? (Defensive: "Why would I steal it? I can synthesize my own.")
   - Fenn? ("I saw Dorian near the tower last night. Just saying.")
   - Dorian? ("Accuse me? My family could BUY this school.")
4. Find evidence: Dorian's practice arena locker contains a potion recipe that requires Phoenix Ash
5. Confront Dorian or report to Thornwick

**Rewards:** +40 XP, Phoenix Ash returned (Thornwick gives you one as thanks), affects Dorian relationship based on how you handle it

### US-AA-54: "The Notice Board"
**As a** student, **I respond** to a notice board posting, **so that** I participate in campus life.

**Trigger:** Ongoing (new postings appear every 3-5 days)

**Notice Board Postings (rotating):**
1. "LOST: One enchanted quill. Answers to the name Gerald. If found, return to Fenn Ashwick. Reward: interesting gossip."
2. "WANTED: Brave student to test new ward configuration. Minor risk of singed eyebrows. — Prof. Ashveil"
3. "HELP: Greenhouse plants have become aggressive. Wear gloves. — Prof. Thornwick"
4. "CHALLENGE: Beat my dungeon time. Level 2, clear all enemies. Current record: 47 turns. — Dorian Blackmere"
5. "SEEKING: Study partner for Divination homework. Will trade Moonwater Vial. — Rod Bumblethatch"

**Rewards:** Vary per posting. 10-30 XP + items or friendship changes.

### US-AA-55: "The Forbidden Section"
**As Elara's friend**, **I help her** access the library's restricted section, **so that** we uncover dangerous knowledge about the school's history.

**Trigger:** Day 20 (requires Elara friendship 4+). Elara asks for help.

**Objectives:**
1. The restricted section is locked. Maren has the only key (around her neck).
2. Three approaches:
   a. **Persuade Maren** (requires social skill check or prior good relationship with Maren — completed "Library Card" quest): Maren reluctantly allows supervised access
   b. **Pick the lock** (Rogue class or lockpick item): Sneak in at night. Risk of being caught.
   c. **Earn access legitimately** (graduate first): Post-graduation, Maren grants access freely
3. Inside: find Celestine Voss's journal and Aldric's research notes
4. Return to Elara with the information

**Rewards:** +60 XP, Elara friendship +3, journal lore entries about the Founding and the Incident, Elara reveals her full theory about cross-school magic

**Consequences:**
- If caught sneaking: Maren is disappointed. Temporary library ban (3 days). Veylen has a stern conversation.
- If Maren allows it: She warns you — "Some of those books change how you think. Literally."

### US-AA-56: "Beneath the Academy"
**As an** adventurous student, **I discover** the ancient chambers beneath the school and encounter the mysterious ward, **so that** the school's deepest secret begins to reveal itself.

**Trigger:** Day 24 (requires either "The Forbidden Section" completed OR discovery of a hidden passage in the practice dungeon level 3)

**Objectives:**
1. Find the hidden passage to the sub-basement (below the practice dungeon)
2. Navigate ancient corridors (different architecture — this predates the Academy by millennia)
3. Encounter the ward: a glowing sigil in the bedrock that hums with power
4. The ward reacts to the player: vision of the Academy's founding, a flash of something ancient
5. Return to the surface

**Rewards:** +80 XP, major lore entry, the player gains a passive buff: "Ley Line Attunement" (+2 max mana permanently)

**Note:** This quest connects to larger game lore (the Original Seven, Ley Lines). It plants seeds for endgame content without resolving anything.

### US-AA-57: "The Academy Tournament"
**As a** student, **I compete** in a spell tournament against fellow students, **so that** my magical growth is tested in a structured competition.

**Trigger:** Day 22. Archmagus Veylen announces the Annual Tournament.

**Objectives:**
1. **Round 1:** vs. Rod (easy — Rod is terrible but tries hard. Beat him gently or crush him.)
2. **Round 2:** vs. Fenn (medium — Fenn is surprisingly competent when he tries: "I've been watching everyone. I know all your tells.")
3. **Round 3:** vs. Elara (hard — she's genuinely skilled. Uses creative spell combinations.)
4. **Finals:** vs. Dorian (very hard — he's trained for this. Uses Firebolt, Arcane Ward, and Frost Lance.)

**Rewards:**
- Win tournament: +100 XP, "Tournament Champion" title, Archmagus gift (choice of rare reagent or spell scroll)
- Runner-up: +60 XP
- Each round won: +20 XP

**Note:** Each opponent has unique post-fight dialogue. Rod congratulates you. Fenn says "I let you win. Mostly." Elara nods silently (respect). Dorian demands a rematch.

## Side Quests

### US-AA-58: "Fenn's Column"
**As Fenn's acquaintance**, **I help** him gather gossip for a notice board "newspaper," **so that** I participate in school social life.

**Trigger:** Day 8 (Fenn approaches player)

**Objectives:**
1. Gather 3 pieces of gossip by talking to different NPCs
2. Choose which items Fenn publishes (some are embarrassing to specific NPCs)
3. See the results posted on the notice board

**Rewards:** +20 XP, Fenn friendship +2, campus mood shifts based on what was published

### US-AA-59: "Rod's Courage"
**As Rod's friend**, **I coach** Rod through a dungeon delve he's been dreading, **so that** Rod grows as a character and the player mentors someone.

**Trigger:** Day 12 (Rod confides he's failing the practical component)

**Objectives:**
1. Accompany Rod into the practice dungeon (Rod follows as a temporary NPC companion)
2. Rod fights alongside you (he's weak but tries — 8 HP, 2 ATK)
3. Help Rod survive 3 floors without dying
4. Rod gains confidence: "I'm never going to be a great mage. But maybe I don't have to be."

**Rewards:** +40 XP, Rod friendship +3, Rod gives you his family heirloom: a shield with +1 HP

### US-AA-60: "The Ghost in the Library"
**As a** curious student, **I investigate** strange sounds in the library at night, **so that** I discover hidden lore about the Sealed East Wing.

**Trigger:** Day 15 (notice board posting or Fenn hint)

**Objectives:**
1. Visit the library after dark (turn count > X for current day)
2. Hear the "ghost" — it's actually the sound of the Sealed East Wing leaking magical energy through the walls
3. Follow the sound to a hidden alcove behind a bookshelf
4. Find an old student journal from 200 years ago (the year of the Incident)
5. Read entries describing Aldric's experiments and the night of the explosion

**Rewards:** +50 XP, Aldric's journal (readable book item), major lore entry

### US-AA-61: "Professor's Pet"
**As a** student, **I assist** one professor with a personal research project, **so that** I build a deeper relationship with faculty.

**Trigger:** Day 18 (choose which professor to help based on who you've impressed most)

**Variants:**
- **Ignis:** Help him test a new fire spell variant. Risk of explosion. Reward: learn `spell_fireball` early (Elements Tier 3 — the only Tier 3 spell teachable at the Academy).
- **Thornwick:** Help tend the greenhouse. His plants are VERY unusual. Reward: 3 rare reagents + greenhouse lore.
- **Ashveil:** Help her test a ward on the Sealed East Wing door. The ward fights back. Reward: Enchantment specialization bonus.
- **Maren:** Help catalog ancient texts. One of them is in a language you don't know... yet. Reward: Deepscript rumor or lore.

**Rewards:** Vary by professor. +40-60 XP + professor-specific bonus.

### US-AA-62: "The Sealed East Wing" (Post-Graduation)
**As a** graduate, **I gain** permission to explore the Sealed East Wing, **so that** the school's greatest mystery is revealed.

**Trigger:** After graduation + completion of "Beneath the Academy" + Elara friendship 5+

**Objectives:**
1. Convince Archmagus Veylen to unseal the wing (requires graduating + proving knowledge of the ward)
2. Enter the East Wing with Elara (she insists on coming)
3. Navigate the time-warped architecture (rooms shift, corridors loop)
4. Encounter Aldric's echo — not a ghost, but a magical imprint of his final moments
5. Learn the truth: Aldric touched the raw Ley Lines. He saw the truth about the Ascended. The explosion was THEM silencing him.
6. Find Aldric's final research notes (a spell that draws from all four schools)
7. Escape as the wing begins to destabilize

**Rewards:** +150 XP, Aldric's Grimoire (unique item), cross-school spell theory (late-game content hook), Elara's permanent friendship (she becomes available as a companion in the overworld)

**Note:** This is endgame Academy content. It connects to the core lore twist (the Ascended's true nature) without resolving it.

## Library Quest Chain

### US-AA-108: "The Lost Collection"
**As a** student who has earned Maren's trust, **I help** the Librarian recover legendary spell tomes that have been lost, stolen, or hidden over the centuries, **so that** the library's knowledge is restored and I learn powerful spells along the way.

**Trigger:** Day 10+ AND Library Card quest (US-AA-50) completed AND at least 2 library books read. Maren approaches: "You've been reading. I mean really reading, not just turning pages to look busy. I need someone who reads like that. The library is... incomplete."

**Overview:** This is a multi-stage side quest spanning the entire school year. Maren reveals that the Academy library once held dozens of spell tomes, but over the centuries many were lost — stolen by expelled students, hidden by paranoid professors, carried off by graduates who never returned them, or deliberately concealed to keep dangerous knowledge from spreading. She has a list. She's been looking for decades. Now she has help.

**Stage 1: "The Overdue Returns" (Day 10-15)**
1. Maren gives the player a list of 3 books she knows are somewhere on campus
2. **"Elemental Convergence" (Ignis's office):** Hidden behind a loose brick where Ignis hides his void-flame notes. Find by examining his office during class time when he's in the Great Hall. Ignis didn't steal it — it was already there when he arrived. Returning it: Maren reads a passage aloud. Teaches `spell_healing_mist` if not already known (Alchemy T1), or `spell_weapon_enchant` if already known (Enchantment T2, +3 ATK 10 turns).
3. **"Thornwick's Private Formulary" (Greenhouse, locked cabinet):** Thornwick borrowed it 30 years ago and "forgot." Confront Thornwick or pick the lock. He blushes: "I was going to return it. I just... wanted to check one more thing. For thirty years." Teaches `recipe_invisibility` (alchemy recipe).
4. **"The Warden's Manual" (Practice dungeon, floor 3):** A former student took it for dungeon practice and died. The book is in a skeleton's hands on floor 3. Teaches `spell_binding_circle` if not already known (Enchantment T1), or `spell_foresight` if already known (Divination T2).

**Rewards (Stage 1):** +60 XP, Maren friendship +2, 1-2 spells depending on what you already know, Maren unlocks the rare books section for you.

**Stage 2: "The Restricted Recovery" (Day 16-22)**
1. Maren reveals that 2 spell tomes were deliberately placed in dangerous locations to keep them "safe"
2. **"Principles of Counter-Magic" (Sub-basement, near the ancient ward):** This book was placed near the ward centuries ago as part of the protective layer. Taking it weakens the secondary ward slightly — Maren knows this and agonizes. She ultimately agrees the knowledge is worth more in students' hands than locked in stone. Teaches `spell_reflective_shield` if not already known (Enchantment T3), or teaches `spell_dispel` if not already known (Enchantment T2). If player knows both: teaches the unique `recipe_fortification` recipe.
3. **"Dreaming and Seeing: Advanced Divination" (Glimmershade common room, hidden in the scrying pool's pedestal):** Lumi notices you searching. If Glimmershade house: she helps freely. If another house: she tests you with a riddle first. "What do you see when you close your eyes but refuse to sleep?" (Answer: "The truth" or "What's really there" or any honest philosophical answer — Lumi is flexible.) Teaches `spell_scryers_mark` if not already known (Divination T2), or `spell_enemy_analysis` (Divination T3, see target's full stats/weaknesses).

**Rewards (Stage 2):** +80 XP, Maren friendship +3, 1-2 spells, lore about the Academy's protective measures, Lumi friendship +1

**Stage 3: "The Legendary Tome" (Day 23+ or post-graduation)**
1. Maren reveals the library's greatest loss: **"Celestine's Primer"** — the founder's personal spell book, containing spells from ALL four Academy schools at Tier 2-3. It was last seen 200 years ago, the night of the Incident.
2. Maren believes it's in the Sealed East Wing. It cannot be recovered until post-graduation (US-AA-62).
3. If the player has already completed the East Wing quest: Maren asks them to return to the wing with her guidance (a shorter delve, different rooms accessed). The Primer is found in Aldric's study — he was reading it when the experiment went wrong.
4. If not yet graduated: Maren notes it in the player's journal as a future goal. "When you're ready. The book has waited two centuries. It can wait a little longer."
5. **Celestine's Primer** is a unique spell tome that teaches ONE spell the player doesn't already know, chosen from: `spell_fireball` (Elements T3), `spell_sanctum` (Enchantment T4), `spell_stone_skin` (Alchemy T3), `spell_premonition` (Divination T4). Costs 2 skill points (legendary tome). The book also contains Celestine's personal notes — deep lore about why she founded the Academy and what she was truly protecting.

**Rewards (Stage 3):** +120 XP, Maren friendship max, 1 high-tier spell (T3-T4), Celestine's Primer (readable lore item), title: "Keeper of the Collection"

**Quest Design Notes:**
- Each stage is self-contained — completing Stage 1 feels rewarding even if Stage 2 is never started
- Books found during this quest teach spells the player doesn't already know (if they've learned the primary option through other means, a secondary option is offered)
- The 1 skill point cost for book-learning (see US-MS-55) applies to all spell tomes found in this quest — the quest rewards the BOOKS, the player still pays the skill point to learn
- Maren's dialogue evolves as you return books: from formal librarian to genuine warmth. By Stage 3 she treats you as a fellow keeper of knowledge, not a student.
- Fenn comments on the quest at each stage: Stage 1: "You're running errands for the librarian? That's either very boring or very interesting. With Maren, probably both." Stage 2: "You're going WHERE for a BOOK? Libraries are supposed to be safe!" Stage 3: "Celestine's actual spell book. Do you understand what that IS? That's... that's history. Actual history. In your hands."

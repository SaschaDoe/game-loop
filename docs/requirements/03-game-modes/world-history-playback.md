# World History Playback

As a player, I want to review a timeline of major world events that have occurred, so that I can understand the history of my world and the consequences of actions.

## Details

- Chronological log of significant events: battles, deaths, quest completions, faction changes
- Events are timestamped with in-game date and location
- Filterable by category: combat, political, economic, personal
- Can "replay" events in a cinematic observer view showing what happened
- Events caused by the player are highlighted differently
- Accessible from the pause menu or observer mode

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Major events are logged automatically during gameplay
- [ ] Timeline is viewable and filterable
- [ ] Player-caused events are visually distinguished
- [ ] Event replay shows the event location and participants

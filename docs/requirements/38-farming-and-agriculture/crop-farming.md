# Crop Farming

As a player, I want to farm crops on owned land near my house, so that I have a peaceful, profitable side activity and a reliable source of cooking ingredients.

## Details

- Farmable land: purchasable plots near player housing, or abandoned farms claimable through quests
- Crop cycle: plant seeds -> water (1 action/day) -> wait 50-150 turns (varies by crop) -> harvest
- Crop types:
  - **Grains** (wheat, barley): fast growing, used in bread and beer, low profit
  - **Vegetables** (potato, carrot, onion): medium growth, cooking staples, moderate profit
  - **Herbs** (healing moss, fire bloom): slow growing, alchemy ingredients, high profit
  - **Rare crops** (moonpetal, void melon): very slow, require special conditions, extremely valuable
- Farming skill: higher skill = better yields, disease resistance, faster growth
- Crop diseases: blight can destroy crops if untreated (herbalism skill helps)
- Seasonal planting: certain crops only grow in certain seasons
- Scarecrow: craftable item that prevents bird damage to crops
- Irrigation: build channels from water sources for auto-watering
- Hire a farmhand NPC to tend crops while you adventure

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Crops are plantable, growable, and harvestable
- [ ] Growth times and seasonal restrictions are enforced
- [ ] Crop diseases can be treated
- [ ] Farmhand NPC tends crops autonomously

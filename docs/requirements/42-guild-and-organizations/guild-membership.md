# Guild Membership

As a player, I want to join guilds and organizations with ranks, benefits, and questlines, so that I have long-term faction goals and access to exclusive content.

## Details

- **Available Guilds**:
  - **Fighters Guild**: combat-focused, arena challenges, bounty board, weapon training
  - **Mages Guild**: spell research, enchanting access, arcane library, magical experiments
  - **Thieves Guild**: stealth missions, fencing stolen goods, lockpick training, black market access
  - **Merchants Guild**: trade routes, bulk discounts, market manipulation, caravan escort quests
  - **Assassins Guild**: (hidden) — kill contracts, poison training, disguise mastery, dark reputation
  - **Healers Circle**: healing magic training, disease curing, free rest at guild halls, resurrection access
- **Rank System** (per guild): Initiate → Member → Veteran → Officer → Master → Guildmaster
- Advancement requires: completing guild quests, paying dues, time-based cooldowns, skill checks
- **Benefits by rank**: access to guild shops, training facilities, safe houses, guild-exclusive spells/abilities, NPC allies
- **Guild conflicts**: some guilds are opposed (Thieves vs Merchants, Assassins vs Healers); joining one may lock or complicate membership in another
- **Guildmaster questline**: becoming Guildmaster triggers a unique storyline for each guild
- Guild halls exist in major towns; each has a notice board with rotating quests
- Expulsion: committing acts against guild principles (e.g., stealing from Merchants Guild members) results in warnings, then expulsion

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All six guilds are joinable with unique questlines
- [ ] Rank progression unlocks appropriate benefits
- [ ] Guild conflicts affect cross-membership correctly
- [ ] Guildmaster storylines are completable
- [ ] Expulsion triggers on code-of-conduct violations

# Secret Societies

As a player, I want to discover and infiltrate hidden secret societies with mysterious agendas, so that the game has deep conspiracy-driven storylines.

## Details

- **Secret Societies** (discovered through rumors, hidden symbols, NPC hints):
  - **The Hollow Eye**: information brokers, know everyone's secrets, trade in blackmail; joining requires bringing them a valuable secret
  - **The Crimson Pact**: blood magic practitioners, seeking to resurrect an ancient god; joining requires a blood sacrifice
  - **The Clockwork Creed**: technology worshippers, building a mechanical god; joining requires bringing them a rare arcane core
  - **The Veil Walkers**: dimensional travelers, mapping alternate realities; joining requires surviving a portal trial
  - **The Last Flame**: apocalypse preppers who believe the world is ending; joining requires proving you've survived a near-death experience
- Each society has: a hidden headquarters, unique currency, exclusive items, a conspiracy questline
- **Double Agent**: possible to infiltrate one society on behalf of another (or on behalf of the law)
- Society membership is hidden from other NPCs; revealing it has consequences
- **Society Quests**: morally ambiguous tasks that may conflict with other quest obligations
- Societies have their own internal politics — factions within factions, power struggles the player can influence
- Discovery: finding society symbols hidden in the world (scratched into walls, hidden in books, on NPCs' hidden tattoos)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All five societies are discoverable through world clues
- [ ] Joining requirements are unique and enforced
- [ ] Double agent gameplay is functional
- [ ] Society questlines have branching outcomes
- [ ] Hidden membership affects NPC interactions when revealed

# Underwater and Vertical Areas

As a player, I want to explore underwater passages and climb vertical shafts, so that the dungeon layouts have more spatial variety beyond flat floors.

## Details

- Underwater areas: require a breathing item or spell, limited turn count before drowning
- Underwater combat: melee range reduced, fire spells disabled, some monsters are amphibious
- Vertical shafts: climbable with rope or climbing skill, falling deals damage
- Multi-level dungeons with visible floors below (transparency effect)
- Flying enemies can attack from above in vertical areas
- Unique underwater and vertical-only treasures and monsters

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Underwater areas enforce breathing mechanics
- [ ] Underwater combat modifications apply
- [ ] Climbing mechanics function with appropriate skill checks
- [ ] Multi-level vertical rendering works correctly

# Hidden NPC Encounters

As a player, I want to discover hidden NPCs in unusual locations who offer unique interactions, so that thorough exploration is rewarded with memorable surprises.

## Details

- **Hidden NPC Types**:
  - **Hermit in the Mountains**: lives in a cave behind a waterfall; offers a unique skill training available nowhere else; speaks in riddles; requires solving his riddle to access training
  - **Ghost at the Crossroads**: appears only at midnight on a specific crossroads; tells the story of their death; completing their unfinished quest grants a spectral weapon
  - **Imprisoned Fairy**: trapped in a bottle on a dusty shelf in a forgotten shop; freeing them grants 1 wish (limited: a rare item, a map to treasure, or a permanent +1 stat)
  - **Underground Merchant**: lives in a hidden cave behind a cracked wall; sells items found nowhere else (weird, powerful, cursed, comedic); accepts barter only (no gold)
  - **The Cartographer**: an old explorer in a remote lighthouse; fills in 25% of your world map for free; sells the rest for gold; knows locations of all secret dungeons
  - **Friendly Monster**: a sentient ogre/troll/goblin who is peaceful; will join as a companion if treated kindly; other NPCs react with fear to them
  - **Time-Lost Traveler**: an NPC from the past/future stuck in the present; offers anachronistic items and confused dialogue; helping them return home grants a time-themed reward
  - **The Collector**: hidden room in a major city; collects rare items; will trade legendary items for specific rare finds; knows the location of every legendary item
- **Discovery Methods**: secret doors, Perception checks, talking to the right NPC, visiting at the right time, carrying a specific item, exploring off the beaten path
- **One-Time Encounters**: some hidden NPCs only appear once; missing them means missing their content for that playthrough
- **World Seed Variation**: hidden NPCs have different locations per world seed; their existence is guaranteed but placement varies
- Hidden NPC encounters grant the "Off the Beaten Path" achievement series

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All hidden NPC types are discoverable through appropriate methods
- [ ] Unique interactions and rewards are specific to each hidden NPC
- [ ] One-time encounters disappear after the interaction
- [ ] World seed varies hidden NPC locations
- [ ] Discovery methods are hinted at but not explicitly stated

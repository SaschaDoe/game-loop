# Cursed Locations

As a player, I want to stumble upon cursed locations where the rules of the world are twisted, so that some areas feel genuinely dangerous and unsettling to explore.

## Details

- Cursed locations are rare areas on the map marked by a subtle visual distortion (color shift, inverted characters)
- Curse effects vary by location:
  - **The Bleeding Forest**: trees drip red, all healing is reversed (healing spells deal damage, damage heals)
  - **The Silent Village**: no sound (audio muted), all text-based communication scrambled, NPCs speak backwards
  - **The Gravity Well**: random gravity shifts — player may fall sideways into walls, enemies walk on ceilings
  - **The Time Loop**: area resets every 20 turns, trapping the player until they find the exit within the loop
  - **The Hunger**: all food in inventory decays instantly upon entering, starvation timer accelerates 10x
  - **The Mirror Marsh**: player's reflection becomes a hostile clone that mimics actions with 1-turn delay
- Breaking the curse: find and destroy the curse source (a relic, a ritual circle, a trapped spirit)
- Reward for breaking curses: powerful items, massive XP, permanent world change (area returns to normal)
- Cursed locations are optional but contain some of the best loot in the game

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Cursed locations have unique visual indicators
- [ ] Curse effects modify gameplay rules as described
- [ ] Curse sources are findable and destroyable
- [ ] Breaking curses provides meaningful rewards

# NPC Relationships

As a player, I want NPCs to have relationships with each other and with me, so that the social fabric of the world has depth and my actions have social consequences.

## Details

- NPCs have relationship scores with each other: friend, rival, spouse, sibling, enemy
- Player has a relationship score with each NPC (hostile to devoted)
- Positive actions (helping, gifting, completing quests) improve relationships
- Negative actions (stealing, attacking, lying) damage relationships
- NPCs gossip: helping one NPC may improve standing with their friends
- Harming an NPC angers their family and friends
- High-relationship NPCs offer discounts, secrets, and unique quests
- Romance options with certain NPCs (tasteful, story-driven)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] NPC-to-NPC relationships exist and are visible
- [ ] Player relationship scores change based on actions
- [ ] Gossip system propagates reputation changes
- [ ] High-relationship NPCs provide tangible benefits

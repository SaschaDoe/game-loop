# NPC Factions

As a player, I want NPCs to belong to different factions with their own goals and rivalries, so that the political landscape of the world creates interesting choices and conflicts.

## Details

- Factions: Thieves Guild, Mages Circle, Holy Order, Merchant League, Shadow Cult, Royal Guard, Rebel Alliance
- Each faction has a headquarters, leader, ranks, and questline
- Player can join multiple factions but some are mutually exclusive
- Faction reputation affects NPC behavior: allies help you, enemies attack on sight
- Faction wars: periodic conflicts between rival factions play out in the world
- Rising in faction ranks unlocks unique abilities, items, and areas

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All factions are present with NPCs, HQ, and leadership
- [ ] Player can join factions and gain rank
- [ ] Mutually exclusive factions block conflicting membership
- [ ] Faction reputation affects NPC disposition

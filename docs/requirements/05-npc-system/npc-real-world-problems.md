# NPC Real World Problems

As a player, I want NPCs to have relatable, real-world-inspired problems and worries, so that they feel human and I empathize with their situations.

## Details

- NPCs deal with problems like:
  - Financial stress: can't afford rent, business is failing
  - Relationship issues: fighting with spouse, estranged children, unrequited love
  - Health concerns: chronic illness, aging, injury recovery
  - Work problems: demanding boss, job dissatisfaction, burnout
  - Grief and loss: mourning a loved one, pet died
  - Social anxiety: fear of public speaking, loneliness, feeling like an outsider
  - Existential questions: meaning of life, fear of death, regret over past choices
- These problems come up naturally in LLM conversations
- Player can offer advice, help practically (fetch medicine, deliver a letter), or just listen
- Helping with personal problems builds deep relationship and unlocks unique rewards
- NPCs remember your help and reference it in future conversations

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each NPC has at least one personal problem
- [ ] Problems surface naturally in LLM dialogue
- [ ] Player can take actions to help (quests, items, conversation)
- [ ] NPCs acknowledge help in subsequent interactions

# NPC Personalities

As a player, I want each NPC to have a distinct personality that affects how they speak and behave, so that conversations feel unique and memorable.

## Details

- Personality traits: friendly, grumpy, nervous, wise, arrogant, mysterious, jovial, paranoid
- Personality affects LLM-generated dialogue tone and word choice
- NPCs have personal interests, fears, and goals that surface in conversation
- Personality influences willingness to help, trade prices, and quest availability
- Some NPCs are compulsive liars; others are brutally honest
- Personality is seeded per NPC and consistent across conversations

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each NPC has assigned personality traits
- [ ] LLM dialogue reflects the NPC's personality consistently
- [ ] Personality affects gameplay interactions (prices, quest access)
- [ ] NPC personality remains consistent across multiple conversations

# NPC Companions

As a player, I want to recruit NPC companions who travel and fight alongside me, so that I have allies in dangerous areas and richer story interactions.

## Details

- Certain NPCs can be recruited after building sufficient relationship or completing their quest
- Companions follow the player, fight enemies, and have their own stats and equipment
- Companions have opinions on player decisions and may leave if pushed too far
- Max party size of 3 (player + 2 companions)
- Companions have unique abilities and dialogue that adds to the story
- Companions can die permanently in combat (optional: revive at temple for a cost)
- Companion AI: configurable behavior (aggressive, defensive, supportive)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Companions can be recruited and dismissed
- [ ] Companions follow player and participate in combat
- [ ] Companion opinions affect relationship and potential departure
- [ ] Companion AI behavior modes are configurable

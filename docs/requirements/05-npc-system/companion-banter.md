# Companion Banter

As a player, I want my companions to talk to each other and comment on the world as we travel, so that the party feels alive and relationships between companions develop.

## Details

- Banter triggers: entering new areas, after combat, discovering lore, during rest, encountering specific NPCs
- Companion-to-companion dialogue: generated by LLM based on both companions' personalities, relationship, and context
- Relationship dynamics: companions can become friends, rivals, or romantic with each other
- Banter topics: commentary on surroundings, teasing each other, debating moral choices, sharing backstories
- Player can interject in banter conversations (LLM includes player as optional participant)
- Frequency: 1 banter per 20-30 turns of travel (not overwhelming)
- Mood-dependent: companions in bad moods argue more; happy companions joke and laugh
- Memorable moments: certain banter lines are saved to a "party memories" journal
- Unique banter combinations: specific companion pairs have exclusive dialogue chains

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Banter triggers contextually during gameplay
- [ ] LLM generates in-character dialogue between companions
- [ ] Companion relationships evolve based on banter interactions
- [ ] Player can participate in banter conversations

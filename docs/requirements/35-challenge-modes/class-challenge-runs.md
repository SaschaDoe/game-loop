# Class Challenge Runs

As a player, I want pre-defined challenge runs with special restrictions and goals, so that I have structured reasons to replay the game differently.

## Details

- Selectable challenges at game start (stackable for harder runs):
  - **Mage Only**: no physical weapons, only spells and staves
  - **Fists of Fury**: no weapons at all, unarmed combat only (special unarmed skill tree unlocks)
  - **Minimalist**: inventory limit of 10 items total
  - **Loner**: no companions allowed, ever
  - **Cursed Start**: begin with a random curse active
  - **Hunted**: a powerful assassin NPC stalks you from the beginning, growing stronger over time
  - **Poverty Vow**: cannot carry more than 100 gold at any time
  - **Blind Run**: fog of war sight radius reduced to 2 tiles
  - **Mute**: cannot initiate dialogue with NPCs (must find other ways to get quests and info)
  - **Chaos Seed**: world seed changes every time you enter a new area (no memorization)
- Each challenge has a unique achievement and cosmetic reward
- Challenge modifiers displayed on the character sheet
- Completing the game with 3+ challenges active simultaneously unlocks the "Ascendant" title

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All challenge modifiers are selectable and stackable
- [ ] Challenge restrictions are enforced throughout gameplay
- [ ] Unique rewards per challenge are granted on completion
- [ ] Multi-challenge completion is tracked

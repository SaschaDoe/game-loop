# Lovecraftian Horrors

As a player, I want to encounter incomprehensible eldritch horrors in the deepest parts of the world, so that late-game exploration has a cosmic horror dimension with unique and terrifying enemies.

## Details

- Eldritch horrors found in: The Void dimension, deepest dungeon floors (50+), cursed ocean depths, behind sealed ancient doors
- Horror types:
  - **The Eye**: a floating mass of eyes that reduces sanity just by being seen, attacks with psychic beams
  - **The Formless**: no fixed shape, changes form each turn, copies a random nearby creature's abilities
  - **Tentacle Swarm**: fills a room with writhing tentacles from every wall, must destroy the core to stop them
  - **The Whisperer**: invisible, only detectable by audio cues, implants paranoia (fake enemy spawns, reversed controls for 1 turn)
  - **The Ancient One** (boss): multi-screen entity, can only damage specific weak points, phases involve fleeing collapsing terrain
- Sanity damage: encountering these creatures reduces sanity (see horror quest system)
- Eldritch resistance: built up over repeated encounters, reduces sanity damage
- Drops: void essence (most powerful crafting material), forbidden knowledge (permanent spell unlocks), sanity-restoring relics
- Lore connection: the eldritch horrors are what the creation myth tried to seal away

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All horror types have unique, unsettling combat mechanics
- [ ] Sanity system integrates with eldritch encounters
- [ ] Eldritch-exclusive drops are functional in crafting
- [ ] The Ancient One boss is a multi-phase spectacle

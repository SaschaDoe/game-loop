# Rare Monster Spawns

As a player, I want to occasionally encounter rare, powerful versions of common monsters, so that exploration has exciting surprise encounters with exceptional rewards.

## Details

- Rare monsters have a 5% chance to spawn instead of a common variant
- Visual distinction: different color, name prefix ("Ancient Goblin", "Shadow Rat", "Enraged Troll")
- Rare monsters have 2x HP, 1.5x damage, and a special ability their common variant lacks
- Guaranteed rare+ loot drop
- Some rare monsters are non-hostile and flee if spotted (catch them for a unique reward)
- A bestiary tracks which rare monsters the player has encountered

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Rare monsters spawn at the correct probability
- [ ] Visual and stat differences are applied
- [ ] Special abilities are unique to the rare variant
- [ ] Bestiary tracks encounters

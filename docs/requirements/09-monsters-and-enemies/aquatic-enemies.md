# Aquatic Enemies

As a player, I want to encounter dangerous aquatic creatures in oceans, lakes, and flooded dungeons, so that water areas have unique and threatening enemy types.

## Details

- **Merfolk Warriors**: intelligent, use tridents and coral armor, can breathe above and below water, territorial
- **Sea Serpent**: massive, multi-tile creature, constrict attack (grapple + crush damage), fast in water
- **Kraken**: boss-tier, tentacles attack from multiple tiles simultaneously, ink cloud (blindness AoE), drag player underwater
- **Piranhas**: swarm enemy, low individual damage but attacks in groups of 10+, frenzy when blood is present
- **Siren**: lures targets with song (Wisdom save or walk toward), drowning damage if lured into deep water
- **Water Elemental**: flooding attacks, absorbs water-based spells, weak to lightning and frost
- **Ghost Ship Crew**: spectral sailors on wrecked ships, phase through objects, chill touch (cold + mana drain)
- **Abyssal Angler**: deep-sea horror, bioluminescent lure, massive bite, found only in ocean depths
- Aquatic combat rules: non-swimming characters move at half speed in water, fire spells disabled, lightning spells deal AoE in water
- Swimming skill: reduces water movement penalty, unlocks underwater combat abilities

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All aquatic types have water-specific behaviors
- [ ] Water combat penalties apply to non-swimmers
- [ ] Fire/lightning spell modifications work in water
- [ ] Aquatic enemies spawn in appropriate water areas

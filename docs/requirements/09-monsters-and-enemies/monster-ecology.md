# Monster Ecology

As a player, I want monsters to exist in a food chain and ecosystem, so that the world feels like a living environment rather than a collection of random enemies.

## Details

- Predator-prey relationships: wolves hunt rats, dragons hunt everything
- Monsters compete for territory: goblin camps and orc camps near each other may war
- Monster populations grow or shrink based on hunting pressure and food availability
- Clearing all wolves from an area causes rat population to explode
- Monster nests/lairs produce new monsters over time if not destroyed
- Dead monsters attract scavengers (vultures, carrion crawlers)
- Observer mode lets you watch the ecosystem play out

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Predator-prey interactions are observable
- [ ] Population dynamics respond to player actions
- [ ] Monster lairs produce new monsters over time
- [ ] Ecosystem changes are visible in observer mode

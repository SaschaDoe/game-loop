# Demon and Celestial Enemies

As a player, I want to fight demons from the Abyss and fallen celestials, so that late-game combat includes the most powerful and lore-significant enemy types.

## Details

- **Demons** (found in the Abyss, summoned by cultists, late-game dungeons):
  - **Imp**: small, fast, throws fireballs, annoying but fragile
  - **Hell Hound**: fire-breathing beast, pack hunter, immune to fire
  - **Succubus/Incubus**: charms targets (Wisdom save), drains stats on hit, shapeshifts
  - **Pit Fiend**: heavily armored, dual-wielding, fear aura, summons imps
  - **Archdemon**: boss-tier, multi-phase fight, unique dialogue taunts the player mid-combat (LLM)
- **Fallen Celestials** (corrupted angels, found in cursed temples and the Void):
  - **Fallen Angel**: holy and shadow magic hybrid, unpredictable attack patterns
  - **Corrupted Seraph**: multi-winged entity, AoE radiance attacks that blind, heals rapidly
  - **The Watcher**: doesn't attack first, observes the player, reflects all damage back until you solve its riddle
- Demons are weak to Holy magic, resistant to Fire and Shadow
- Celestials are resistant to all magic but vulnerable to physical attacks
- Both types drop planar essence (crafting material for endgame enchantments)
- Lore ties: demons and celestials are connected to the creation myth and the main storyline

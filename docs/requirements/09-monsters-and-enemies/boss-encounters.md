# Boss Encounters

As a player, I want to face challenging boss enemies with unique mechanics, so that major milestones feel epic and require skill to overcome.

## Details

- Bosses guard key locations: dungeon depths, faction strongholds, story-critical areas
- Each boss has:
  - A name and lore background
  - Multiple attack phases (changes behavior at HP thresholds)
  - A unique mechanic (e.g., summons minions, becomes invulnerable temporarily, charges up devastating attack)
  - A larger ASCII representation (2x2 or 3x3 tiles)
  - Special loot drops (legendary artifacts, rare materials)
- Boss arenas have environmental features the player can use tactically
- Defeat animation and victory fanfare in ASCII art
- Example bosses: The Hollow King (undead), Magmaw the Devourer (volcanic), The Weaver (spider queen)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Boss encounters have multi-phase mechanics
- [ ] Boss-specific arenas are generated
- [ ] Unique loot drops on defeat
- [ ] Victory presentation displays correctly

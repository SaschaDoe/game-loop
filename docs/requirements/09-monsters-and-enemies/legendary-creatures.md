# Legendary Creatures

As a player, I want to encounter legendary one-of-a-kind creatures roaming the world, so that the most dangerous hunts feel mythic and their defeat is a story worth telling.

## Details

- 6-8 unique legendary creatures, each with a name and lore:
  - **Zephyros the Storm Wyrm**: massive dragon in the mountain peaks, controls lightning and wind
  - **The Pale Devourer**: an albino basilisk in the deep swamp, petrifies with its gaze
  - **Ironhide the Unbreakable**: an ancient armored golem in the ruins, immune to physical damage (magic only)
  - **The Whisper Queen**: a spectral spider in the shadow caves, controls illusions and fear
  - **Magmaw the Undying**: a lava serpent in the volcano, regenerates unless killed by frost
  - **The Nameless One**: a shapeshifter that takes the form of a trusted NPC before revealing itself
- Each legendary creature has a dedicated mini-questline to locate and weaken it
- Defeating a legendary creature permanently changes the world (the storm clears, the swamp drains, etc.)
- Each drops a unique legendary artifact and unlocks an achievement
- Legendary creatures can only be fought once per playthrough

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All legendary creatures are implemented with unique mechanics
- [ ] Mini-questlines lead to each creature
- [ ] World state changes on defeat
- [ ] Unique artifact drops on kill

# Cold Case Files

As a player, I want to investigate old unsolved crimes that reveal long-buried secrets, so that detective work connects to the world's history and lore.

## Details

- **Cold Case Sources**: dusty case files in guard stations, old newspaper archives, ghost testimony, NPC elders mentioning unsolved incidents
- **Case Types**:
  - **Disappearance**: someone vanished 50+ years ago; trail is cold; must find witnesses who are now old or dead (ghost dialogue); may uncover a living criminal who's been hiding in plain sight
  - **Unsolved Murder**: a victim whose killer was never found; evidence is degraded; forensic magic can restore partial clues; often connected to current political power
  - **Lost Treasure**: a famous theft that was never recovered; the thief left coded clues that only make sense with modern knowledge; treasure is still hidden
  - **Conspiracy**: a seemingly simple old case that, when investigated, reveals a larger cover-up involving a current faction or powerful NPC
  - **Wrongful Conviction**: someone was punished for a crime they didn't commit; proving their innocence now (posthumously or to free a still-living prisoner) reveals the real culprit
- **Investigation Challenges**:
  - Evidence decay: physical evidence degrades with time; magical restoration partially recovers it
  - Dead witnesses: use ghost dialogue (haunted locations), séance, or necromancy to interview the dead
  - Changed landscape: locations from the crime may have been rebuilt, destroyed, or overgrown; cross-reference with old maps
  - Coverup layers: powerful NPCs who benefit from the case staying unsolved actively obstruct your investigation
- **Rewards**: substantial gold, unique historical items, lore revelations, NPC gratitude (descendants of victims), justice reputation boost
- **Case Board**: dedicated section in the journal for cold cases; pin evidence, draw connections, track leads
- **Time Limit**: none — cold cases can be worked on at any pace; but some leads expire if NPCs die of old age before you talk to them

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All cold case types are discoverable and investigable
- [ ] Evidence degradation creates meaningful investigation challenges
- [ ] Ghost/necromancy witness interviews work via LLM
- [ ] Cover-up obstruction creates dynamic opposition
- [ ] Case board tracks evidence and connections

# Alignment System

As a player, I want my choices to shift my moral alignment along good/evil and lawful/chaotic axes, so that my character develops a moral identity that the world reacts to.

## Details

- Two-axis alignment: Good-Evil (vertical) and Lawful-Chaotic (horizontal)
- Actions shift alignment: helping NPCs (good), stealing (evil/chaotic), obeying laws (lawful), breaking rules (chaotic)
- Alignment affects: NPC reactions, available quests, faction access, dialogue options, spell availability
- Certain items only usable by specific alignments (holy sword requires Good, shadow dagger requires Evil)
- Alignment is visible in the character sheet as a position on a 2D grid
- Extreme alignment unlocks unique titles and abilities:
  - Paragon (extreme good): healing aura, NPC devotion
  - Tyrant (extreme evil): fear aura, dark magic access
  - Arbiter (extreme lawful): diplomatic immunity, command NPCs
  - Anarchist (extreme chaotic): unpredictable crits, rule-breaking abilities
- Alignment shifts gradually; sudden shifts require dramatic acts

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Alignment shifts based on player actions
- [ ] NPC reactions change with alignment
- [ ] Alignment-locked items and spells are enforced
- [ ] Extreme alignment abilities are unlockable

# Learn Rumors and Secrets

As a player, I want to learn rumors, secrets, and useful information from NPCs through conversation, so that exploration and quests benefit from social interaction.

## Details

- NPCs know local rumors: hidden treasures, monster sightings, faction conflicts
- Rumors are generated per NPC based on their location, profession, and social connections
- Some rumors are true, some are exaggerated, some are outright false
- Buying drinks at taverns loosens NPC tongues (increased rumor chance)
- Secrets are harder to extract: require high relationship, persuasion, or bribery
- Learned rumors/secrets are logged in the player's journal
- Acting on rumors can lead to hidden quests and locations

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] NPCs share rumors during conversation
- [ ] Rumors vary in accuracy (true, exaggerated, false)
- [ ] Tavern interaction increases rumor sharing
- [ ] Learned information appears in the journal

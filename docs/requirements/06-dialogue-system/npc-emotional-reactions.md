# NPC Emotional Reactions

As a player, I want NPCs to react emotionally to what I say and do, so that conversations feel human and my words have weight.

## Details

- NPCs have emotional states: happy, sad, angry, fearful, surprised, disgusted, neutral
- Emotional state shifts based on dialogue content and player actions
- Emotional state affects: dialogue tone, willingness to help, combat behavior (angry NPCs may attack)
- Visual indicators show NPC emotion (ASCII emoticons or color changes on their tile)
- Extreme emotional reactions can trigger events: an NPC storms off, breaks down crying, or attacks
- Emotional recovery: NPCs calm down over time or with player apology

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] NPC emotional state changes based on interaction
- [ ] Emotional state visibly affects NPC behavior and dialogue
- [ ] Extreme emotions trigger appropriate events
- [ ] Emotions recover over time

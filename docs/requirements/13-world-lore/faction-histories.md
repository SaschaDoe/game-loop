# Faction Histories

As a player, I want each faction to have a rich backstory explaining their origins and motivations, so that faction conflicts feel grounded and meaningful.

## Details

- Each faction has: founding story, key historical events, heroes and villains, current goals
- Faction history is revealed through: quest dialogue, lore books, NPC conversations, faction HQ exploration
- Historical events explain current rivalries (e.g., the Mages and the Holy Order split after a magical catastrophe)
- Player can visit historical sites mentioned in faction lore
- Faction history influences how NPCs of that faction talk about others

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each faction has written lore accessible in-game
- [ ] Historical sites are explorable locations
- [ ] NPC dialogue references faction history
- [ ] Lore is discoverable through multiple channels

# Ancient Languages

As a player, I want to discover and learn ancient languages to read inscriptions, understand old texts, and unlock hidden content, so that linguistic puzzles add a scholarly dimension.

## Details

- **Language System**: 6 ancient languages exist in the world, each tied to a civilization:
  - **Draconic**: language of dragons and dragon-worshippers; found in dragon lairs, volcanic temples; unlocks dragon dialogue
  - **Elvish (Ancient)**: pre-modern elven script; found in deep forests, ancient ruins; unlocks hidden elven magic
  - **Dwarvish (Runic)**: runic inscriptions in dwarven mines and fortresses; unlocks crafting schematics and trap mechanisms
  - **Abyssal**: language of demons and the underworld; found in cursed areas, forbidden tomes; unlocks dark magic and demon negotiation
  - **Celestial**: language of divine beings; found in temples, holy relics, angelic encounters; unlocks healing and protection prayers
  - **Primordial**: language of elementals and the first beings; found in elemental shrines, creation sites; unlocks weather and nature magic
- **Learning Process**:
  - Find inscriptions (displayed as garbled ASCII symbols until partially learned)
  - Each inscription provides a fragment of understanding (+5% comprehension of that language)
  - At 25%: basic meaning (nouns and verbs); at 50%: full meaning of simple texts; at 75%: complex texts and subtle nuances; at 100%: fluent, can write in the language
  - Scholars and linguist NPCs can teach languages for gold (speeds up learning)
  - Rosetta Stone-type artifacts: found rarely, instantly grant +25% to a language
- **Encrypted Content**: some quest-critical information is in ancient languages; must learn enough to read it
- **Linguistic Puzzles**: door locks that require speaking/writing in ancient languages; riddles in dead tongues; passwords in Draconic
- **Cross-Language Roots**: knowing one language helps with related ones (Ancient Elvish helps with Celestial at 50% transfer)
- **Language Journal**: tracks all discovered inscriptions, comprehension percentages, and translated texts

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All six languages have discoverable inscriptions throughout the world
- [ ] Comprehension percentage gates readability at correct thresholds
- [ ] NPC teachers and artifacts accelerate language learning
- [ ] Linguistic puzzles require appropriate language knowledge
- [ ] Cross-language bonus applies between related languages

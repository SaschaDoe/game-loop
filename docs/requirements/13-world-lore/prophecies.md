# Prophecies

As a player, I want to discover cryptic prophecies that foreshadow events and hint at my destiny, so that the narrative has a mystical, epic quality.

## Details

- Prophecies are found in temples, spoken by oracle NPCs, or written in ancient texts
- Written in cryptic, poetic language that hints at future quests and events
- Some prophecies refer to the player directly ("A stranger from beyond will wield the flame...")
- Prophecies become clearer as the player progresses through the story
- Fulfilling a prophecy triggers a special event or unlocks a unique reward
- Multiple prophecies form a larger meta-narrative when pieced together
- LLM-powered oracle NPCs can interpret prophecies when asked

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Prophecies are discoverable in the world
- [ ] Prophecy text is cryptic but solvable
- [ ] Fulfillment triggers special events
- [ ] Oracle NPCs provide LLM-powered interpretations

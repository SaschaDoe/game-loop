# Legendary Heroes of the Past

As a player, I want the world to have a history of legendary heroes whose deeds are remembered, so that I can follow in their footsteps and find their lost relics.

## Details

- 5-6 legendary heroes from the world's past:
  - **Aelindra the Starweaver**: an elven archmage who sealed the Shadow Gate — her staff is lost in the ruins
  - **Borik Ironhand**: a dwarven king who forged the Adamantine Throne — his hammer lies in a collapsed mine
  - **The Silver Fox**: a legendary thief who stole from the gods — their dagger is hidden behind the hardest puzzle
  - **Seraphina the Undying**: a paladin who sacrificed herself to banish a lich — her armor exists in the Ghost Realm
  - **Grimjaw the Conqueror**: an orc warlord who united the tribes — his war horn summons spectral warriors
- Hero stories are told by bards, written in books, carved in monuments
- Questlines to find each hero's lost artifact
- Ghost encounters with the heroes themselves in special locations
- Heroes' choices in the past created the current political landscape

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each hero has discoverable lore across multiple sources
- [ ] Artifact questlines are completable
- [ ] Ghost encounters with heroes offer unique dialogue
- [ ] Past events visibly shaped the current world

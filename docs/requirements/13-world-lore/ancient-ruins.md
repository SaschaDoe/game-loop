# Ancient Ruins

As a player, I want to discover ancient ruins that predate the current civilization, so that the world feels old and layered with hidden history.

## Details

- Ruins are special dungeon areas with unique tile sets (crumbling stone, overgrown vines)
- Contain powerful but old-world enemies (golems, spirits, constructs)
- Inscriptions in ancient languages (see language barriers user story)
- House some of the most powerful artifacts and lore
- Environmental puzzles based on ancient mechanisms
- Ruins tell the story of a fallen civilization through environmental storytelling
- Some ruins are hidden and only discoverable through lore hints or quests

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Ruins have unique visual theming
- [ ] Ancient enemies populate ruins
- [ ] Inscriptions and puzzles are functional
- [ ] Ruins contain high-tier rewards

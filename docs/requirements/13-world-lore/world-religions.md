# World Religions

As a player, I want the world to have multiple religions with temples, priests, and believers, so that the spiritual dimension of the world adds depth and quest opportunities.

## Details

- At least 4 religions:
  - **Church of the Radiant Sun**: mainstream good-aligned faith, healing, anti-undead
  - **Cult of the Void**: secretive dark religion, shadow magic, forbidden knowledge
  - **The Old Ways**: nature worship, druids, balance, shapeshifting
  - **The Clockwork Creed**: worship of logic and constructs, anti-magic, artificing
- Each religion has: temples, priests, holy texts, rituals, and a deity/philosophy
- Player can worship a deity for passive bonuses and access to deity-specific prayers/abilities
- Cleric and Paladin classes must choose a religion at creation
- Temples offer services: healing, curse removal, blessings, resurrection
- Religious conflicts create quest opportunities (crusades, heresies, forbidden rituals)
- Praying at shrines in the wild grants temporary buffs aligned with the deity

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All religions have temples, NPCs, and lore
- [ ] Worship grants passive bonuses
- [ ] Temple services are functional
- [ ] Religious conflicts generate quests

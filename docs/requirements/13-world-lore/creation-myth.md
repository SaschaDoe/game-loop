# Creation Myth

As a player, I want the game world to have a creation myth that explains its origins, so that the lore has depth and the world feels like it has history.

## Details

- A founding myth involving ancient gods, primordial forces, or a cosmic event
- The creation myth explains: why dungeons exist, why monsters roam, why magic works
- Fragments of the myth are discoverable: temple inscriptions, ancient scrolls, NPC elders
- The main quest connects to the creation myth (an ancient evil returning, a prophecy fulfilling)
- Different races have different versions of the creation myth (cultural perspectives)
- Complete discovery of all myth fragments unlocks an achievement and bonus lore

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Creation myth content is written and distributed across the world
- [ ] Myth fragments are discoverable in multiple locations
- [ ] Main story references the creation myth
- [ ] Achievement triggers on complete collection

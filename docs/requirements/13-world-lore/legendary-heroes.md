# Legendary Heroes of the Past

As a player, I want the world to have legendary heroes from history whose deeds shaped the land, so that I can discover their stories, find their relics, and follow in their footsteps.

## Details

- **Legendary Heroes** (5-8 per world seed, randomly generated from archetypes):
  - **The Unbroken Knight**: paladin who held a bridge alone against an army; their shield is hidden in the ruins of that bridge
  - **The Starweaver**: mage who wove the constellations into spells; their staff is in an astronomical observatory dungeon
  - **The Silent Shadow**: assassin who killed a tyrant king; their blade is in the tyrant's sealed tomb
  - **The Beast Speaker**: ranger who brokered peace between humans and dragons; their bow is in a dragon's hoard
  - **The Laughing Bard**: musician whose song ended a war; their lute is in a forgotten tavern beneath a battlefield
- **Hero Shrines**: found throughout the world, contain lore tablets telling parts of each hero's story
- **Relic Quests**: piecing together a hero's full story from shrine fragments reveals the location of their legendary item
- **Hero's Legacy**: wielding a legendary hero's relic unlocks a unique ability tied to their story
- **NPC Knowledge**: bards and scholars know hero tales; LLM dialogue can reveal shrine locations and story details
- **Hero Ghosts**: at certain shrines during specific conditions (full moon, solstice), the hero's ghost appears for dialogue
- Legendary heroes may have connections to current factions (descendants, orders founded in their name)
- Completing all hero quests unlocks "Hall of Legends" — a hidden area with the combined history and a unique reward

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Legendary heroes are generated with unique stories per seed
- [ ] Hero shrines contain discoverable lore fragments
- [ ] Relic quests lead to legendary items
- [ ] Legacy abilities activate when wielding relics
- [ ] Hero ghosts appear under correct conditions

# Spirit World

As a player, I want to enter a spirit world to communicate with ancestors and nature spirits, so that the world's spiritual dimension offers unique quests, lore, and abilities.

## Details

- Spirit world accessible through: shaman NPCs, sacred groves, meditation skill, spirit gate artifacts
- Visual style: translucent overlay on the normal map — physical world visible but faded, spirits visible and glowing
- Spirit types:
  - **Ancestor spirits**: deceased NPCs who offer wisdom, quests, and blessings
  - **Nature spirits**: tied to locations (river spirit, mountain spirit, forest spirit), offer protection or curses
  - **Lost souls**: trapped spirits needing help to pass on (escort quests, solving their unfinished business)
  - **Spirit guardians**: powerful entities protecting sacred sites, must be appeased or defeated
- Spirit world mechanics: physical attacks don't work (only magic and spirit weapons), spirits can pass through walls
- Spirit quests: help spirits resolve their regrets, earn spirit tokens for unique rewards
- Spirit companion: befriend a spirit that provides passive bonuses (ancestor's battle instinct: +2 dodge)
- Shamanic abilities: class-agnostic skills earned through spirit world interaction (spirit sight, ghost touch, ethereal step)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Spirit world renders as a visual overlay
- [ ] All spirit types are interactable
- [ ] Spirit-specific combat rules apply
- [ ] Spirit quests provide unique rewards

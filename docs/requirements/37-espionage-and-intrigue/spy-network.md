# Spy Network

As a player, I want to build a network of informants and spies across towns, so that I can gather intelligence, manipulate events, and gain information advantages.

## Details

- Recruit informants: bribe NPCs, help them with personal problems, place allies in key positions
- Informant types:
  - **Tavern spy**: overhears conversations, reports rumors and faction movements
  - **Merchant contact**: reports on trade routes, incoming caravans, price changes
  - **Guard insider**: warns of arrests, bounty changes, guard patrol schedules
  - **Court spy**: reveals political decisions before they're public, faction alliances
  - **Thieves Guild mole**: reports on criminal activity, planned heists, black market stock
- Spy network management screen: map view showing all informants, their location, reliability, and recent intel
- Intel quality: unreliable informants sometimes provide false information
- Counter-espionage: enemy factions may discover your spies (loyalty check), captured spies reveal your activities
- Assassin contracts: high-level spy network can arrange "accidents" for targeted NPCs (evil alignment shift)
- Cost: informants require gold upkeep per 100 turns; unpaid spies go silent or flip sides

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Informants are recruitable across towns
- [ ] Intel reports appear in the message feed or journal
- [ ] Unreliable informants sometimes provide false info
- [ ] Counter-espionage risks are functional

# Vigilante Justice

As a player, I want to operate outside the law as a vigilante, punishing criminals the justice system won't touch, so that there's a morally gray path between lawful and criminal.

## Details

- **Vigilante Actions**:
  - **Target Identification**: discover criminals the law can't or won't prosecute (corrupt officials, bribed judges, untouchable nobles, hidden crime lords)
  - **Evidence Gathering**: collect proof of their crimes through investigation, espionage, and informant networks
  - **Confrontation**: choose to expose them publicly (reputation + legal consequences), blackmail them (gold + ongoing leverage), or deliver vigilante justice (combat, non-lethal or lethal)
  - **Rescue Missions**: save victims the guards have given up on (kidnapping victims, enslaved NPCs, framed innocents)
- **Vigilante Reputation**: separate hidden reputation; tracked by criminals (who fear you) and law enforcement (who consider you a criminal)
  - High vigilante rep with criminals: thugs surrender when they see you; crime bosses put bounties on you
  - High vigilante rep with citizens: common folk leave gifts at your doorstep; NPCs protect your identity
  - High vigilante rep with guards: some guards secretly support you; others double down on catching you
- **Secret Identity**: vigilante actions should be done in disguise; if your true identity is linked to vigilante activity, you face arrest + wanted level increase
- **Vigilante Gear**: unique items obtained through vigilante questline:
  - Shadow Mask: conceals identity, +3 intimidation
  - Grappling Hook: rapid rooftop traversal
  - Smoke Pellets: instant escape (guaranteed flee from combat)
  - Calling Card: leave at the scene; builds vigilante legend
- **Moral Spectrum**: choose how far to go — some vigilantes only expose, others blackmail, others kill; NPC reactions scale with your methods
- **Nemesis Criminal**: a master criminal who opposes you specifically; grows in power as you take down their organization; final confrontation is a major questline

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All vigilante action types function correctly
- [ ] Vigilante reputation tracks separately from normal reputation
- [ ] Secret identity system tracks exposure risk
- [ ] Vigilante gear provides unique gameplay advantages
- [ ] Nemesis criminal scales and confrontation is completable

# Overworld Map

As a player, I want an overworld map connecting dungeons, towns, and points of interest, so that I can choose where to explore and plan my journey.

## Details

- Top-down ASCII overworld with terrain tiles (grass, mountains, rivers, roads)
- Towns, dungeons, and landmarks are placed on the overworld
- Roads connect major locations for faster travel
- Rivers and mountains act as natural barriers requiring items or abilities to cross
- Fog of war: unexplored areas are hidden until visited
- Random encounters can occur while traveling between locations

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Overworld renders with distinct terrain types
- [ ] Locations are discoverable and enterable
- [ ] Fog of war hides unexplored areas
- [ ] Random encounters trigger during overworld travel

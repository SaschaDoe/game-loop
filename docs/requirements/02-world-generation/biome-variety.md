# Biome Variety

As a player, I want to explore different biomes in the overworld, so that the world feels large, diverse, and worth exploring.

## Details

- At least 6 biomes: Forest, Desert, Tundra, Swamp, Mountains, Volcanic Wasteland
- Each biome has unique ASCII tile sets and color palettes
- Biomes affect gameplay: movement speed, visibility, encounter types
- Biomes have unique ambient flavor text and environmental events
- Transitions between biomes are gradual, not abrupt
- Certain resources, monsters, and NPCs are biome-specific

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All biomes are visually distinct in ASCII rendering
- [ ] Biome-specific mechanics are functional (e.g., slower movement in swamp)
- [ ] Biome transitions render smoothly
- [ ] Biome-specific content spawns correctly

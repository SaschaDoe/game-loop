# Haunted Locations

As a player, I want to discover haunted buildings, forests, and battlefields where ghosts linger, so that the world has spooky locations with unique encounters tied to the area's tragic history.

## Details

- Haunted locations types:
  - **Haunted Mansion**: abandoned noble house, poltergeist activity (objects thrown at player), ghost of the owner with unfinished business
  - **Haunted Battlefield**: site of an ancient war, spectral soldiers fight endlessly, reliving the battle
  - **Haunted Forest**: trees move when you're not looking, will-o'-wisps lead you astray, a dryad spirit guards a secret
  - **Haunted Mine**: cave-in killed miners, ghost miners still dig, mine cart traps, gas pockets
  - **Haunted Ship**: wrecked vessel, ghost crew follows their routines, captain has a treasure map
- Visual indicators: flickering tile colors, cold breath visual (blue particles), items moving on their own
- Ghosts are interactable: LLM dialogue to learn their story and find out what keeps them bound
- Resolution: solve the ghost's unfinished business to put them to rest (reward: spectral items, lore, XP)
- Unresolved hauntings: ghosts become more aggressive over time, spreading the haunting to nearby areas
- Haunted locations are more dangerous at night (more ghosts, stronger effects)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All haunted location types are discoverable
- [ ] Visual haunting effects render correctly
- [ ] Ghost dialogue reveals their backstory
- [ ] Resolution quests are completable for rewards

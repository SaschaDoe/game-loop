# Player-Run Shops

As a player, I want to set up my own shop in a town to sell items to NPCs, so that I can become a merchant and earn passive income.

## Details

- Rent a market stall in any town (cost scales with town size)
- Stock the stall with items from your inventory and set prices
- NPCs browse and buy items over time based on demand and your pricing
- Hire an NPC shopkeeper to run it while you adventure
- Shop reputation grows with fair prices and good stock; attracts more customers
- Overpricing drives customers away; underpricing attracts bargain hunters but reduces profit
- Specialty shops earn more: a potion shop near a dungeon entrance, a weapon shop in a military town
- Daily sales report viewable from anywhere (sent via courier NPC or journal update)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Market stalls are rentable and stockable
- [ ] NPCs buy items autonomously over time
- [ ] Pricing affects customer volume
- [ ] Sales reports are accessible remotely

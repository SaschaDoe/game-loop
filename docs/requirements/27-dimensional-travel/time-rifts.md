# Time Rifts

As a player, I want to discover time rifts that transport me to past or future versions of familiar locations, so that exploration has a temporal dimension.

## Details

- **Time Rift Types**:
  - **Past Rifts**: travel 100-1000 years into the past; see current ruins as they were when intact, meet historical NPCs, witness events from lore books
  - **Future Rifts**: travel 100-1000 years forward; see current towns in ruins or evolved into cities, meet descendants of current NPCs, encounter advanced/degraded technology
  - **Temporal Echoes**: brief glimpses (5 turns) of a past event overlaid on the current map; observe only, cannot interact
- **Past World Interactions**:
  - Talk to historical figures via LLM dialogue (they don't know you're from the future)
  - Witness the events described in lore books firsthand
  - Retrieve items from the past (they appear as "ancient" versions in the present)
  - WARNING: changing major past events causes temporal paradoxes (unpredictable world changes when you return)
- **Future World Features**:
  - See consequences of your current actions projected forward
  - Advanced enemies (higher level, new abilities)
  - Future loot: items that don't exist yet in the present (futuristic enchantments)
  - Overgrown and ruined versions of familiar maps
- **Temporal Paradox**: if you significantly change the past, returning to the present causes "temporal storm" — the world shifts (NPC changes, building changes, quest changes); higher magnitude changes = more disruption
- **Time-Locked Items**: some items only work in their native time period; others transcend time (legendary artifacts)
- **Chronomancer Quests**: a hidden order of time mages offers quests involving fixing temporal anomalies caused by other time travelers
- Time rifts are rare (3-5 per world) and discoverable through chronomancy spells or ancient maps

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Past rifts generate historically appropriate versions of locations
- [ ] Future rifts project plausible future states
- [ ] Temporal paradox system alters the present based on past changes
- [ ] LLM dialogue works with historical NPCs
- [ ] Time-locked items enforce time period restrictions

# Portal Network

As a player, I want to discover and activate ancient portals that connect distant locations, so that I can fast-travel through a mysterious arcane network.

## Details

- Portals are ancient stone archways found in ruins, dungeons, and hidden groves
- Each portal must be activated by solving a puzzle or spending a resource (mana crystal)
- Once activated, a portal permanently links to the network and becomes a fast-travel point
- Portal travel is instant but costs a small mana fee
- Some portals are one-way or lead to trapped dimensions (must find the exit)
- Portal map: a dedicated screen showing all discovered portals and connections
- Unstable portals: randomly shift destination, dropping you in unexpected locations
- Portal guardians: ancient constructs that must be defeated before activation

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Portals are discoverable and activatable
- [ ] Activated portals enable fast travel between linked locations
- [ ] One-way and unstable portals behave correctly
- [ ] Portal guardians must be defeated before activation

# Alternate Dimensions

As a player, I want to travel to alternate dimensions with different rules and aesthetics, so that late-game exploration offers radically different environments.

## Details

- Dimensions accessible through special portals, quest events, or artifacts:
  - **The Fae Realm**: enchanted forest dimension, time moves differently (1 turn = 10 in the real world), trickster NPCs, illusion-heavy puzzles
  - **The Abyss**: demonic dimension, fire and brimstone, every creature is hostile, gravity shifts periodically
  - **The Clockwork Plane**: mechanical dimension, construct enemies, gear-based puzzles, no magic works (physical combat only)
  - **The Dream Nexus**: surreal dimension where thoughts become real, player stats fluctuate randomly, LLM NPCs speak in riddles
  - **The Void**: empty dimension, perfect darkness, extreme danger, but contains the rarest resources and lore
- Each dimension has unique rules that override normal gameplay (e.g., no magic, inverted controls, permadeath regardless of mode)
- Dimension-exclusive materials, monsters, and artifacts
- Time spent in other dimensions affects the real world (NPCs age, events progress)
- Returning from a dimension may drop you at a different location than where you entered

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All dimensions are accessible and have unique rulesets
- [ ] Dimension-exclusive content exists
- [ ] Time differential affects the main world
- [ ] Return mechanics work correctly

# Fishing

As a player, I want to fish in rivers, lakes, and the ocean, so that I can gather food, find rare items, and enjoy a peaceful side activity.

## Details

- Fishing requires a fishing rod (purchasable or craftable) and bait
- Cast line at water tiles; wait 1-5 turns for a bite
- Fish types vary by location: freshwater (trout, bass), saltwater (tuna, swordfish), magical (glowfish, void eel)
- Rare catches: treasure chests, enchanted items, monster encounters (sea serpent!)
- Fish can be: eaten raw (low benefit), cooked (better stats), sold to merchants, used in alchemy
- Fishing skill improves with practice; higher skill = rarer catches
- Fishing contests at festival events
- Peaceful ambient: fishing near NPCs can trigger casual conversation (LLM small talk)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Fishing mechanic works at water tiles
- [ ] Fish variety differs by location
- [ ] Rare catches include items and encounters
- [ ] Fishing skill progression affects catch quality

# Trading Between Players

As a player, I want to trade items and gold with other players, so that we can help each other and build an in-game economy.

## Details

- Trade initiated by standing adjacent to another player and using the trade action
- Both players see a trade window showing offered items and gold
- Both players must confirm before the trade executes
- Trade history logged for dispute resolution
- Anti-scam protection: items are verified before trade completes
- Cannot trade quest items or soul-bound artifacts
- Optional: player-run market stalls in towns

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Trade interface displays both players' offers
- [ ] Both-party confirmation is required
- [ ] Trade restrictions on quest/bound items are enforced
- [ ] Trade history is logged

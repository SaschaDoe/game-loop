# Ghost Realm

As a player, I want to briefly explore a ghost realm between death and respawn, so that dying itself becomes an interesting gameplay moment with hidden content.

## Details

- On death, player enters the Ghost Realm for 10-20 turns before respawning
- Ghost Realm is a shadowy, inverted version of the area where you died
- Can see and talk to other dead NPCs and adventurers (LLM dialogue about their deaths)
- Hidden collectibles only accessible in the Ghost Realm (ghost coins, spectral items)
- A mysterious NPC "The Reaper" offers bargains: trade max HP for instant respawn, or accept a curse for a powerful boon
- Ghost Realm has unique monsters that can only be fought here
- Optional: find a secret exit to return to life without the respawn penalty
- Lore about the afterlife and the creation myth is discoverable here

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Ghost Realm loads as an alternate map after death
- [ ] Ghost NPCs are interactable with LLM dialogue
- [ ] Ghost-exclusive collectibles and monsters exist
- [ ] The Reaper's bargains function correctly

# Beast Forms

As a player, I want lycanthropy to include multiple beast types beyond werewolves, each with unique abilities, so that lycanthropic infection offers variety.

## Details

- **Lycanthrope Types**:
  - **Werewolf**: balanced combat form; STR/DEX focus; pack hunter; most common; found in forests and wilderness
  - **Werebear**: tank form; massive STR/CON boost (+7 STR, +5 CON); slow but devastating; maul attack (stun + heavy damage); hibernation heal (full HP recovery, 10-turn vulnerability); found in mountains and tundra
  - **Weretiger**: agile predator; DEX/STR focus (+4 DEX, +5 STR); pounce (leap 5 tiles + pin); stealth in tall grass/jungle; rake attack (rapid claw combo); found in jungles and tropical forests
  - **Wererat**: stealth form; DEX/INT focus (+5 DEX, +2 INT); small size (fit through grates, under doors); disease bite (infects target with filth fever); sewer navigation bonus; found in cities and sewers
  - **Werebat**: aerial form; DEX/WIS focus (+3 DEX, +4 WIS); echolocation (see in darkness, detect invisible); glide (not true flight but long jumps); sonic screech (AoE stun); found in caves and underground
  - **Wereshark**: aquatic form; STR/CON focus (+6 STR, +4 CON); water breathing; blood frenzy (damage increases as enemy HP lowers); bite (massive single damage); only transforms near large bodies of water
  - **Werecrow**: scout form; INT/WIS focus (+3 INT, +4 WIS); flight; mimic voice (repeat any heard phrase, useful for deception); murder of crows summon (AoE harassment); found near battlefields and graveyards
- **Infection Source**: each beast type transmits its specific lycanthropy; bitten by a werebear = become a werebear; cannot have multiple lycanthropy types simultaneously
- **Hybrid Form**: at high lycanthropy mastery (500+ turns as lycanthrope), unlock a hybrid humanoid-beast form; retains human intelligence and equipment use while gaining beast abilities at 50% power; the most versatile but least powerful form
- **Beast Rivalry**: different lycanthrope types are territorial; werewolves and werebears are natural enemies; joining one faction makes others hostile; rare diplomatic players can broker peace

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All beast forms have correct stat modifications and unique abilities
- [ ] Infection correctly transmits the specific lycanthropy type
- [ ] Hybrid form unlocks at correct mastery threshold with correct power scaling
- [ ] Beast rivalries create appropriate faction tensions
- [ ] Each beast type's habitat affects where encounters occur

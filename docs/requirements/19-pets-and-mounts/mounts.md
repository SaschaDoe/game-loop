# Mounts

As a player, I want to ride mounts for faster overworld travel, so that long journeys are quicker and I can access mounted combat.

## Details

- Mount types: horse (fast, common), war horse (combat-capable), giant spider (climbs walls), griffin (flies over terrain)
- Mounts double overworld movement speed
- Mounted combat: bonus to charge attacks, penalty to spellcasting
- Mounts have their own HP; dismounted if the mount takes too much damage
- Mounts cannot enter dungeons or buildings (auto-dismount, mount waits outside)
- Purchasable at stables or earnable through quests
- Mount equipment: saddles, barding (armor), saddlebags (extra inventory space)
- Mount symbol overlays player symbol on the map when riding

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Mounts increase overworld travel speed
- [ ] Mounted combat bonuses and penalties apply
- [ ] Auto-dismount at dungeon/building entrances
- [ ] Mount equipment is equippable and functional

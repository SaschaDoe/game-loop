# Player Housing

As a player, I want to own a house or base that I can customize and use as a hub, so that I have a permanent home in the world for storage, crafting, and display.

## Details

- Purchasable houses in towns (price scales with town size and location)
- House interior is a customizable map: place furniture, crafting stations, storage chests
- Trophy wall: display defeated boss heads, legendary weapons, rare finds
- Bed provides a free full rest with no ambush risk
- Personal stash: unlimited storage accessible only from your house
- Upgradeable: small hut -> house -> manor -> castle (each upgrade costs gold and materials)
- Hire NPC staff: a guard, a cook (auto-generates food), a librarian (stores lore books)
- House is a fast-travel destination

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Houses are purchasable in towns
- [ ] Interior is customizable with furniture placement
- [ ] Storage, crafting, and rest all function in the house
- [ ] Upgrades visually change the house and add capacity

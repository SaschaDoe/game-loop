# Tamed Pets

As a player, I want to tame wild creatures as loyal pets that follow me and assist in combat, so that I have an animal companion that grows alongside my character.

## Details

- Tameable creatures: wolves, hawks, cats, snakes, spiders, baby dragons (rare)
- Taming requires: food the creature likes + Animal Handling skill check
- Failed taming may anger the creature (attacks) or cause it to flee
- Pets have: loyalty meter, hunger, HP, a basic attack, and a special ability
- Pet special abilities: wolf howl (stuns enemies), hawk scout (reveals map area), cat stealth (detects traps)
- Pets level up with the player, gaining stats
- Pets can die in combat; revivable at a druid NPC or with a rare item
- Maximum 1 active pet (can store others at a stable)
- Pet names are customizable; NPCs comment on your pet

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Taming mechanic works with skill checks
- [ ] Pets follow the player and fight in combat
- [ ] Pet special abilities are functional
- [ ] Pets level up and can be revived

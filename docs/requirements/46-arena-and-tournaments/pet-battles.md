# Pet Battles

As a player, I want to enter my tamed pets into battles against other pets, so that pet training has a competitive outlet with unique rewards.

## Details

- **Pet Battle Arenas**: found in major towns; entry fee + pet must be at least Juvenile evolution stage
- **Battle Format**:
  - 1v1: single pet fight, best of 3 rounds
  - Team: 3v3 pet teams, switch between rounds, last team with a conscious pet wins
  - Survival: single pet vs waves of NPC pets; see how many wins before defeat
- **Pet Combat Mechanics**:
  - Pets use their own abilities (element-specific attacks, special moves based on evolution)
  - Player issues commands: Attack (choose ability), Defend (reduce incoming damage), Special (unique move, limited uses), Swap (team battles)
  - Speed stat determines turn order
  - Type advantages: fire > nature > water > fire; shadow > light > shadow; physical has no advantage/disadvantage
- **Training**: between battles, train pets to improve specific stats (attack training dummy, agility course, sparring partner)
- **Pet Moves**: each pet learns up to 4 active moves; new moves learned at evolution stages; can only equip 4 (choose which to keep)
- **Tournament Bracket**: weekly pet tournament with escalating difficulty; grand prize is a rare egg (hatches into a unique pet)
- **Betting**: spectators bet on matches; player can bet on their own pet or others
- **Pet Grooming**: well-groomed pets get a +1 bonus in pet battles (cosmetic care = stat bonus)
- **Rivalries**: recurring NPC pet trainers with named pets and evolving strategies; defeating all rivals unlocks the Champion title

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All battle formats (1v1, team, survival) function correctly
- [ ] Pet combat uses correct ability and type advantage mechanics
- [ ] Training improves pet stats between battles
- [ ] Tournament bracket progresses with escalating difficulty
- [ ] Rival NPC trainers recur with memory of past battles

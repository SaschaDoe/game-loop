# Gladiator Career

As a player, I want to pursue a full gladiator career from slave fighter to arena champion with themed matches, sponsors, and crowd management, so that arena combat is a deep progression system.

## Details

- **Career Entry**: voluntarily sign up at an arena (free agent); be captured and forced to fight (slave path); or be sentenced to arena combat (criminal path); each path has different starting conditions and narrative
- **Career Ranks**: Meat (expendable novice) > Contender (regular fighter) > Favorite (crowd darling) > Champion (arena master) > Legend (retired title, NPC trainers reference you)
- **Match Types**:
  - **Solo Duel**: 1v1 against another gladiator; classic; crowd judges both skill and showmanship
  - **Team Battle**: 2v2 or 3v3; coordinate with ally gladiators; team chemistry matters (fight with the same partner repeatedly for combo bonuses)
  - **Beast Hunt**: fight exotic monsters released into the arena; crowd loves spectacle; harder monsters = more fame
  - **Gauntlet**: fight 5 opponents in sequence without rest between; tests endurance and resource management
  - **Execution Match**: fight a condemned prisoner; player can choose mercy (crowd reaction varies — some boo, some cheer; sponsor reaction varies)
  - **Exhibition**: non-lethal showfighting; pure showmanship; crowd entertainment is the only metric; no death risk
- **Crowd Favor**: tracked separately from combat performance; crowd loves: dramatic finishes, flashy moves, taunting opponents, playing to the audience; crowd hates: quick boring kills, cowardice, excessive brutality (context-dependent)
- **Sponsors**: wealthy NPCs sponsor gladiators; provide better equipment, healing between matches, and gold stipends; in return, must wear their colors and fight when told; multiple sponsors compete for top gladiators
- **Signature Move**: develop a trademark finishing move; name it; crowd chants for it; using it in a match dramatically boosts crowd favor; signature move has a cooldown (1 per match)
- **Freedom**: slave gladiators can earn freedom by reaching Champion rank; freed gladiators can continue fighting for gold or retire; retiring at Legend rank grants permanent +2 CHA and arena-related dialogue options everywhere

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Career ranks advance based on wins and crowd favor
- [ ] All match types produce correct combat scenarios
- [ ] Crowd favor responds to correct showmanship actions
- [ ] Sponsor system provides equipment and stipends with obligations
- [ ] Freedom mechanic unlocks at Champion rank for slave-path characters

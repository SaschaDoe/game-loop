# Minimap

As a player, I want a minimap in the corner of the screen showing my explored surroundings, so that I can navigate without opening the full map.

## Details

- Small overview map in the top-right corner
- Shows explored terrain, player position, quest markers, and NPC locations
- Color-coded dots for different entity types
- Toggleable on/off with a hotkey
- Click on minimap to open full map view centered on that location
- Minimap respects fog of war

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Minimap renders explored areas correctly
- [ ] Player position and markers are visible
- [ ] Fog of war is respected
- [ ] Minimap can be toggled and clicked to open full map

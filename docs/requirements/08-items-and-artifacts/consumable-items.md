# Consumable Items

As a player, I want to find and use consumable items like potions, scrolls, and food, so that I have tactical options and resource management during exploration and combat.

## Details

- Potions: health, mana, strength boost, speed boost, invisibility, poison cure
- Scrolls: single-use spells (fireball scroll, teleport scroll, identify scroll)
- Food: restores hunger meter and provides minor buffs (see survival mechanics)
- Thrown items: bombs, acid flasks, smoke bombs (AoE effects)
- Consumables have weight and take inventory space
- Potions can be mixed by alchemist NPCs for custom effects
- Unidentified potions have randomized colors per playthrough

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All consumable types are functional
- [ ] Consumables are removed from inventory on use
- [ ] Unidentified potions have randomized appearances
- [ ] Custom potion mixing produces valid effects

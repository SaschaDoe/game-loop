# Item Enchanting

As a player, I want to enchant my weapons and armor with magical properties, so that I can customize my gear with bonus effects.

## Details

- Enchanting requires: enchanting table, soul gems (from defeated enemies), and gold
- Enchantments: fire damage, frost damage, life steal, mana steal, thorns, fortify stat
- Each item can hold 1-2 enchantments depending on rarity
- Higher-level soul gems produce stronger enchantments
- Enchantments can be removed and replaced (destroys the old enchantment)
- Failed enchanting can destroy the item (low risk with high skill)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Enchanting interface allows selecting item, enchantment, and soul gem
- [ ] Enchanted items gain the specified bonus effects
- [ ] Enchantment limits per item are enforced
- [ ] Soul gems are consumed on use

# Cursed Items

As a player, I want some items to be cursed with hidden drawbacks, so that loot has an element of risk and I need to be cautious about equipping unknown items.

## Details

- Cursed items appear as normal or even attractive items until equipped
- Once equipped, cursed items cannot be removed without visiting a temple or using a Remove Curse spell
- Curse effects: stat reduction, damage over time, attracts enemies, inverts controls
- Identify spell or NPC appraiser can reveal curses before equipping
- Some cursed items are powerful but come with a known trade-off (risk vs reward)
- Cursed items are more common in deeper dungeons and suspicious locations

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Cursed items appear unidentified until equipped or identified
- [ ] Curse effects activate and persist until removed
- [ ] Remove Curse mechanics work (temple, spell)
- [ ] Identify reveals curse status before equipping

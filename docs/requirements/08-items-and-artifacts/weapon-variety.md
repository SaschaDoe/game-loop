# Weapon Variety

As a player, I want a wide variety of weapons with different stats and playstyles, so that I can find gear that suits my build and enjoy the thrill of upgrades.

## Details

- Weapon categories: swords, axes, maces, daggers, bows, crossbows, staves, spears, whips
- Rarity tiers: Common (grey), Uncommon (green), Rare (blue), Epic (purple), Legendary (orange)
- Higher rarity = better base stats + bonus properties
- Weapons have: damage range, speed, crit chance, special effects
- Two-handed weapons deal more damage but prevent shield use
- Weapon requirements: minimum Strength or Dexterity to equip

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All weapon categories are represented with multiple variants
- [ ] Rarity tiers are visually distinct and affect stats
- [ ] Weapon requirements are enforced
- [ ] Weapon properties affect combat calculations

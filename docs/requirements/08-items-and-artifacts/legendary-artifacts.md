# Legendary Artifacts

As a player, I want to discover legendary artifacts with unique powers and rich lore, so that finding rare items feels like a momentous event that changes my capabilities.

## Details

- 15-20 unique legendary artifacts in the game, each with a name, backstory, and unique ability
- Examples:
  - **Shadowfang**: dagger that turns the wielder invisible for 3 turns after a kill
  - **Crown of the Lich King**: grants necromancy spells but slowly drains max HP
  - **Aegis of Dawn**: shield that reflects magic projectiles back at the caster
  - **Boots of the Wind Walker**: doubles movement speed, allows crossing water
  - **Orb of Omniscience**: reveals the entire map and all hidden enemies
  - **The Hungering Blade**: grows stronger with each enemy killed (stacking damage buff)
- Artifacts are found in hidden locations, boss drops, or as quest rewards
- Each artifact has a discovery cutscene (ASCII art + lore text)
- Some artifacts have drawbacks (cursed artifacts)
- Artifacts cannot be bought or sold; they are bound to the player

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All artifacts are findable in the game world
- [ ] Unique abilities function correctly
- [ ] Lore text and discovery presentation displays properly
- [ ] Artifacts with drawbacks apply penalties

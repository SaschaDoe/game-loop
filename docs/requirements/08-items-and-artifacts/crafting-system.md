# Crafting System

As a player, I want to craft items from gathered materials, so that I can create gear tailored to my needs rather than relying solely on found loot.

## Details

- Gather materials from the world: ore, herbs, leather, wood, gems, monster parts
- Crafting stations: forge (weapons/armor), alchemy table (potions), enchanting table (enchantments)
- Recipes are discovered by: finding recipe scrolls, experimenting, or learning from NPC crafters
- Crafted item quality depends on materials used and player's crafting skill level
- Rare materials produce better results (mithril ore, dragon scales, moonpetal)
- Failed crafting attempts consume some materials

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Materials are gatherable from the world
- [ ] Crafting stations produce items from valid recipes
- [ ] Material quality affects output quality
- [ ] Recipes are learnable through multiple paths

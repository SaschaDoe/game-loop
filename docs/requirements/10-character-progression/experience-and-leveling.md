# Experience and Leveling

As a player, I want to earn experience points from combat, quests, and exploration, so that my character grows stronger and I feel a sense of progression.

## Details

- XP gained from: killing monsters, completing quests, discovering locations, solving puzzles
- Level-up thresholds increase with each level (exponential curve)
- On level-up: increase max HP and mana, gain skill points, unlock new abilities
- Level-up notification with stat increase summary
- XP bar displayed in the HUD
- Level cap of 50 with endgame content balanced for max level

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] XP is awarded from all valid sources
- [ ] Level-up occurs at the correct thresholds
- [ ] Stats increase and skill points are granted on level-up
- [ ] XP bar and level display correctly in HUD

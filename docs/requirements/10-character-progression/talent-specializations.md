# Talent Specializations

As a player, I want to unlock unique talents through gameplay achievements rather than just leveling, so that my playstyle earns me specialized abilities.

## Details

- Talents are earned by performing specific actions repeatedly:
  - **Berserker Rage**: unlocked after killing 50 enemies while below 25% HP — grants damage boost at low HP
  - **Silent Killer**: unlocked after 20 stealth kills — backstab damage permanently increased
  - **Arcane Mastery**: unlocked after casting 100 spells — mana costs reduced by 15%
  - **Iron Stomach**: unlocked after eating 30 different food types — immunity to food poisoning
  - **Silver Tongue**: unlocked after 20 successful persuasion checks — permanent Charisma +2
  - **Trapsmith**: unlocked after disarming 25 traps — can craft advanced traps
  - **Undying Will**: unlocked after surviving 10 deaths — bonus HP on respawn
  - **Beast Whisperer**: unlocked after taming 5 different pets — taming success rate doubled
  - **Treasure Sense**: unlocked after opening 50 chests — hidden chests glow faintly
  - **Marathon Runner**: unlocked after traveling 10,000 tiles — movement costs no hunger
- Talents are permanent once unlocked
- Talent tracker shows progress toward each talent in a dedicated panel
- Some talents are hidden until nearly unlocked (surprise discovery)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Talents unlock when conditions are met
- [ ] Talent effects apply permanently
- [ ] Progress tracker shows advancement
- [ ] Hidden talents reveal near completion

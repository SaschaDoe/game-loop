# Hunger and Thirst

As a player, I want hunger and thirst meters that drain over time, so that I must manage food and water supplies adding a survival layer to exploration.

## Details

- Hunger and thirst decrease with each turn (configurable rate)
- Stages: satisfied, hungry, starving (HP loss per turn), dehydrated (stat penalties)
- Food sources: rations, hunted meat, foraged berries, tavern meals, NPC gifts
- Water sources: wells, rivers, water skins, purchased drinks
- Cooking system: raw ingredients can be combined for better meals with buffs
- Hunger/thirst can be disabled in settings for players who prefer pure adventure
- Certain races have reduced hunger/thirst requirements

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Hunger and thirst decrease over time
- [ ] Negative effects apply at low levels
- [ ] Food and water sources restore meters
- [ ] System can be disabled in settings

# Environmental Dangers

As a player, I want the environment itself to pose dangers like extreme cold, heat, and toxic areas, so that biome traversal requires preparation and the right gear.

## Details

- Cold (tundra, mountains): stamina drains faster, movement slowed without warm clothing
- Heat (desert, volcanic): dehydration accelerates, fire resistance reduces damage
- Toxic (swamp, sewer): poison damage over time without a gas mask or antidote
- Darkness (deep caves): movement without light source risks falling into pits
- Altitude (mountain peaks): reduced stamina, requires climbing gear
- Environmental protection items: fur cloak, desert wrap, gas mask, lantern, climbing boots
- NPCs warn about environmental dangers in nearby areas

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Environmental effects apply based on biome
- [ ] Protection items mitigate effects
- [ ] Unprepared travel into harsh biomes has consequences
- [ ] NPC warnings are available before entering dangerous biomes

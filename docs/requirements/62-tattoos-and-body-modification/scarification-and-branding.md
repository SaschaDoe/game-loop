# Scarification and Branding

As a player, I want ritual scarification and branding to provide unique bonuses tied to faction allegiance and personal sacrifice, so that permanent body marks carry weight and meaning.

## Details

- **Scarification Rituals**: performed by shamans, cult leaders, or self-inflicted; costs HP during the process; CON save determines scar quality
- **Brand Types**:
  - **Faction Brands**: each major faction offers a unique brand mark; grants faction-specific bonus (+10% faction reputation gain, access to faction safe houses, faction ability); cannot be hidden; marks you as a member permanently
  - **Slave Brands**: given when captured by slavers; -5 CHA with free citizens; recognized by other slaves for solidarity; can be removed but leaves visible scar
  - **Criminal Brands**: court-ordered punishment; marks thieves (hand brand), murderers (face brand), traitors (chest brand); -reputation with law-abiding NPCs; +reputation with criminals
  - **Warrior Scars**: ritual scars earned through combat feats (first kill, boss defeat, survival of near-death); each grants a small permanent bonus (+1 STR, +1 CON, intimidation bonus)
  - **Holy Brands**: temple-bestowed marks of devotion; grants minor divine power (heal 1/day, turn undead bonus, resist dark magic); requires maintaining piety or brand fades
  - **Curse Marks**: inflicted by enemies, curses, or dark rituals; debuffs until cured; visible indicator of corruption; some grow and spread over time
- **Scar Stories**: each scar/brand has a tooltip describing how it was obtained; contributes to character history
- **Social Impact**: guards check brands at city gates; some establishments refuse branded individuals; brand-specific dialogue options
- **Scar Removal**: divine healing (expensive), alchemical treatment (partial), illusion magic (temporary concealment); some brands resist removal

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All brand types apply correct bonuses and penalties
- [ ] Faction brands correctly gate faction content
- [ ] Criminal brands affect NPC interactions appropriately
- [ ] Warrior scars accumulate from correct triggers
- [ ] Scar removal options work with appropriate difficulty

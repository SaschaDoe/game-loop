# Tower Defense Dungeon

As a player, I want a dungeon where I must defend a position against waves of enemies using traps, turrets, and positioning, so that combat has a strategic base-defense variant.

## Details

- Triggered by specific quest or location (defend the gate, protect the artifact)
- Setup phase: place traps, barricades, and turrets along the path enemies will take
- Wave system: 5-10 waves of increasing size and strength
- Between waves: repair, reposition, and purchase additional defenses with gold
- Turret types: arrow turret (single target, fast), cannon (AoE, slow), magic orb (elemental damage), healing totem (heals nearby allies)
- Barricades: redirect enemy pathing, have HP and can be destroyed
- Player fights alongside defenses: position yourself at chokepoints for maximum impact
- Companions and recruited soldiers man additional positions
- Bonus objective: defend without taking any damage to the protected target for extra rewards
- Final wave: mini-boss leading the assault with special abilities

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Setup phase allows trap and turret placement
- [ ] Enemy waves spawn and path correctly
- [ ] Between-wave purchasing and repair works
- [ ] Bonus objectives track and reward correctly

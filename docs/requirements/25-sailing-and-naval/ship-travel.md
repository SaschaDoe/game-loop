# Ship Travel

As a player, I want to buy or earn a ship to sail across oceans and discover islands, so that exploration extends beyond land into a vast maritime world.

## Details

- Ship types: rowboat (slow, free, rivers only), sloop (small, fast, 1 crew), galleon (large, slow, cargo space), warship (armed, requires crew)
- Ships are purchasable at port towns or earnable through pirate/navy questlines
- Sailing uses a simplified overworld map with ocean tiles, islands, reefs, and currents
- Wind direction affects travel speed (displayed as an arrow on the HUD)
- Navigation skill: higher skill reveals hidden islands on the sea map and avoids reefs
- Ship inventory: separate cargo hold for trading goods
- Docking at ports: access island towns, hidden coves, and underwater dungeon entrances
- Ship customization: name your ship, upgrade hull, add cannons, improve sails

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Ships are purchasable and boardable
- [ ] Ocean overworld map is navigable
- [ ] Wind and current mechanics affect travel
- [ ] Ship inventory and customization work

# Island Discovery

As a player, I want to discover uncharted islands with unique biomes, dungeons, and inhabitants, so that sailing rewards me with exclusive content not found on the mainland.

## Details

- Hidden islands not visible until sailed near or revealed by treasure maps
- Island types:
  - **Tropical Paradise**: friendly natives, rare fruits, relaxation buffs
  - **Skull Island**: pirate stronghold, deadly traps, massive treasure hoard
  - **Volcanic Isle**: fire-themed dungeon, obsidian ore, fire elemental boss
  - **Ghost Ship Graveyard**: wrecked ships, undead sailors, spectral loot
  - **The Living Island**: the island itself is a creature; entire surface shifts and changes
  - **Scholar's Retreat**: ancient library, spell scrolls, sage NPC who answers one question truthfully
- Each island has a unique landmark and discoverable lore
- Some islands are only accessible during certain weather or seasons
- Treasure maps found on the mainland point to specific islands

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Hidden islands are discoverable through exploration and maps
- [ ] Each island has unique content and theming
- [ ] Conditional access (weather, season) is enforced
- [ ] Treasure maps lead to correct island locations

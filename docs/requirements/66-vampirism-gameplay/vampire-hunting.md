# Vampire Hunting

As a player, I want to hunt vampires as a mortal using specialized tools and tactics, so that non-vampire characters have engaging anti-vampire gameplay.

## Details

- **Becoming a Hunter**: join the Van Helsing Guild (requires WIS 14+ and completing an initiation quest); or go freelance after witnessing a vampire attack; hunters gain access to vampire-specific equipment and abilities
- **Hunter Equipment**:
  - Stakes: wooden stakes deal massive damage to vampires; instant kill if used during a grapple on a prone vampire (stake through the heart)
  - Holy Water Vials: thrown weapon; burns vampires (3x damage); splash radius hits adjacent tiles
  - Garlic Bombs: AoE that creates a garlic cloud (5 turns); vampires in the cloud are nauseated (-3 all stats) and can't use special powers
  - Silver-Edged Weapons: deal bonus damage to vampires and prevent regeneration
  - Sunlight Orb: magical device that creates artificial sunlight in a room; devastating to vampires; expensive and single-use
  - UV Lantern: continuous sunlight in a small radius; keeps vampires at bay; requires fuel (sunstone shards)
  - Mirror: reveals vampires (no reflection); also deflects Mesmerize attempts
- **Tracking Vampires**: investigation skill to identify vampire signs (no reflection, cold skin, no heartbeat, avoiding garlic vendor areas); follow blood trails; interrogate thralls; check crypts during daytime
- **Vampire Nests**: locate and assault vampire lairs during the day (vampires sleeping, weakened); or set traps at night; nests have thrall guards and magical wards
- **Bounty System**: cities post vampire bounties when attacks increase; higher bounties for older/more powerful vampires; proof required (fangs, ash, signet ring)
- **Moral Complexity**: some vampires are peaceful, only feeding on animals or willing donors; hunting them is an evil act; player must decide which vampires deserve death
- **Hunter Abilities**: gained through guild training:
  - Sense Undead: detect vampires within 15 tiles
  - Iron Will: advantage on saves vs Mesmerize/Dominate
  - Consecrated Strike: infuse weapon with holy energy for 1 attack (2x damage vs undead)
  - Death's Vigil: immune to fear effects from undead

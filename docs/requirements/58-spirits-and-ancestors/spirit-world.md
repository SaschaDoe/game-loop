# Spirit World

As a player, I want to enter a parallel spirit world overlapping the physical one, so that shamanic characters can see hidden truths and interact with spiritual entities.

## Details

- **Spirit World Access**: through shamanic trance (meditation + spirit herbs), at spirit gates (thin points between worlds), or automatically during certain world events (equinox, eclipses)
- **Spirit World Properties**:
  - Overlays the real world map; same layout but different contents (walls may not exist in the spirit world; new paths appear; living creatures appear as their soul-forms)
  - Colors are inverted; spirit world has a blue/silver tint to all tiles
  - Time moves differently: 1 spirit turn = 0.5 real turns (spirit world is faster)
  - No physical objects exist; only spiritual representations (a locked chest in the real world may be a sleeping guardian spirit)
- **Spirit Entities**:
  - **Nature Spirits**: trees, rivers, and mountains have guardian spirits; can be bargained with for boons (nature magic boost, safe passage, weather control)
  - **Lost Souls**: spirits of the dead who haven't moved on; some are confused, some are aggressive, some are trapped
  - **Spirit Predators**: malicious entities that feed on souls; attack the player's spiritual form; damage carries to the physical body
  - **The Spirit Council**: a group of ancient spirits who govern the spirit world; audience with them requires a quest chain; they can grant blessings or curses that affect the physical world
- **Spirit Vision** (passive, lower cost): see the spirit world overlaid on the physical world without fully entering; reveals: hidden objects, NPC true natures (kind NPCs glow, evil ones are dark), spiritual corruption in an area
- **Shamanic Quests**: spirit entities offer quests that must be completed in the spirit world (cleanse corruption, guide lost souls, defeat spirit predators, restore a nature spirit's domain)
- **Physical Vulnerability**: while in the spirit world, your physical body is defenseless; companions can guard it, or find a safe location first
- **Spirit World Crafting**: some ingredients only exist in the spirit world (soul flowers, ether dust, spirit bone); used for shamanic gear and potions

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Spirit world generates as an overlay of the physical world correctly
- [ ] All spirit entity types are interactable with unique behaviors
- [ ] Spirit vision reveals hidden information without full entry
- [ ] Physical body vulnerability creates strategic decisions
- [ ] Spirit world crafting ingredients are gatherable and usable

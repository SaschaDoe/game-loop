# Trophy Collection

As a player, I want to collect trophies from defeated monsters that I can display, trade, or use in crafting, so that every hunt leaves a tangible reward beyond XP and gold.

## Details

- **Trophy Drops**: each monster type drops a unique trophy item on defeat (dragon scale, goblin ear, troll tooth, demon horn, etc.); drop chance varies (common enemies 30%, bosses 100%, rare enemies 50%)
- **Trophy Types**:
  - **Body Parts**: claws, fangs, horns, scales, eyes, hearts; used in alchemy and crafting
  - **Essence**: magical residue from arcane/elemental creatures; used in enchanting and spell research
  - **Pelts/Hides**: from beasts; used in leather crafting and decoration
  - **Skulls**: mounted for display; rare trophies for powerful enemies
  - **Unique Trophies**: one-of-a-kind drops from bosses (dragon's crown, lich's phylactery, kraken's eye); quest items or legendary crafting materials
- **Trophy Display**: mount trophies on walls in player housing; each displayed trophy provides a small passive bonus (dragon scale on wall = +2% fire resist in the house area); visitors can see your collection
- **Trophy Hall**: a dedicated room in player housing; completing trophy sets (all goblinoid trophies, all dragon trophies, all undead trophies) unlocks a set bonus (permanent stat bonus, title, unique ability)
- **Trophy Trading**: sell trophies to collectors, alchemists, or enchanters; rare trophies command high prices; some NPCs request specific trophies for quests
- **Bestiary Link**: collecting a trophy from a creature type adds detailed information to the bestiary entry (weaknesses, resistances, behavior patterns); complete trophy = complete bestiary entry
- **Trophy Degradation**: trophies stored improperly degrade over time; requires preservation kit or display case; degraded trophies sell for less and lose crafting quality

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each monster type drops its unique trophy at correct drop rates
- [ ] Trophy display provides correct passive bonuses
- [ ] Trophy sets track completion and grant set bonuses
- [ ] Bestiary entries update when trophies are collected
- [ ] Trophy degradation occurs without proper storage

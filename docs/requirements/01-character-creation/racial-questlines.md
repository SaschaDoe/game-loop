# Racial Questlines

As a player, I want each race to have a unique questline tied to their heritage, so that my race choice unlocks exclusive story content and a powerful racial ability.

## Details

- Each race has a 3-quest chain triggered at levels 5, 15, and 25:
  - **Human — Legacy of the First Kingdom**: uncover your noble bloodline, reclaim a lost ancestral keep, choose to restore the monarchy or establish a republic. Reward: "Adaptability" — once per combat, copy any enemy ability you've seen
  - **Elf — The Fading**: investigate why elves are losing their connection to magic, journey to the World Tree, heal or sever the bond. Reward: "Timeless" — aging effects and time magic don't affect you, +3 to all magic
  - **Dwarf — The Deep Forge**: rediscover the legendary forge of your ancestors, gather mythic ores, relight the forge. Reward: "Mastercraft" — all crafted items are automatically +1 rarity
  - **Orc — Blood of the Warchief**: prove your lineage through combat trials, unite the scattered orc clans, challenge the false chief. Reward: "Battle Fury" — gain +1 ATK for every enemy killed in the current combat (stacks)
  - **Halfling — The Lucky Ones**: investigate your people's supernatural luck, infiltrate a casino rigged against halflings, challenge Fate itself. Reward: "Fortune's Favorite" — reroll any failed check once per encounter
  - **Tiefling — Chains of the Pact**: confront the demon your ancestor made a deal with, descend into the Abyss, break or renegotiate the pact. Reward: "Infernal Heritage" — fire immunity + one free demon summon per day
- Questlines are optional but deeply rewarding
- NPCs of the same race comment on your quest progress

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each race has a completable 3-quest chain
- [ ] Racial abilities are granted on questline completion
- [ ] Choices in the questline produce distinct outcomes
- [ ] Same-race NPCs acknowledge quest progress

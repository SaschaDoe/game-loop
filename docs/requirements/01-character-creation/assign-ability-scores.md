# Assign Ability Scores

As a player, I want to distribute ability score points across my character's attributes, so that I can customize my character's strengths and weaknesses.

## Details

- Six core attributes: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma
- Point-buy system with a pool of distributable points
- Attributes affect derived stats (HP, mana, carry weight, dodge chance, etc.)
- Racial bonuses are applied on top of assigned scores
- Show a summary of how each attribute affects gameplay
- Optional: offer a "random roll" for players who prefer randomness

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Player can allocate points across all 6 attributes
- [ ] Point total is enforced (cannot exceed budget)
- [ ] Racial bonuses are applied after allocation
- [ ] Derived stats update in real-time as points are assigned

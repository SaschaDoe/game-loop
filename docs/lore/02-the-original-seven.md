# The Original Seven — The True First Gods

## Nature of the Original Seven

The Original Seven were not beings in the way mortals understand. They were **living principles** — aspects of reality given awareness. They did not have bodies, names (the names used here are mortal approximations of their resonance frequencies), or desires. They simply *maintained* reality by existing.

When they sacrificed themselves to cage the Void Serpent, they became the Ley Lines — the invisible network of magical energy that holds the world together. They are not dead. They are not alive. They are the world itself.

---

## The Seven Principles

### 1. Aum-Varek — The Principle of Order

**Domain:** Structure, law, pattern, mathematics, crystalline perfection

**Nature:** Aum-Varek was the reason that 2+2=4, that crystals form in hexagons, that orbits are elliptical. Without Aum-Varek, reality would be random noise.

**Sacrifice:** Became the deep-earth Ley Lines that maintain geological stability. Volcanoes erupt when Aum-Varek's lines are disrupted. Earthquakes happen when the pattern strains.

**Remnant Magic:**
- Rune magic (imposing order through symbols) draws on Aum-Varek's residue
- Wards and protective circles are echoes of Aum-Varek's caging of the Void Serpent
- Dwarven runic crafting unknowingly channels this principle

**Modern Echo:** Scholars who study mathematics sometimes feel a presence guiding them — not a voice, but a *certainty* that the answer exists and is beautiful. This is Aum-Varek.

---

### 2. Kha-Siel — The Principle of Change

**Domain:** Transformation, adaptation, evolution, entropy (the constructive kind), seasons

**Nature:** Kha-Siel was the reason that caterpillars become butterflies, that children grow, that ice melts and water boils. Without Kha-Siel, the universe would be frozen in its first instant forever.

**Sacrifice:** Became the atmospheric and oceanic Ley Lines — the currents of air and water that drive weather and seasons. Unnatural weather indicates disruption of Kha-Siel's lines.

**Remnant Magic:**
- Transmutation magic (turning lead to gold, etc.) is Kha-Siel's echo
- Shapeshifting and polymorphism tap directly into this principle
- The druidic cycle of seasons is a ritual celebration of Kha-Siel's sacrifice

**Modern Echo:** The feeling of standing at a crossroads and sensing that *something* wants you to choose the path of growth — that's Kha-Siel, still whispering through the wind.

---

### 3. Tho-Rienne — The Principle of Time

**Domain:** Past, present, future, causality, memory, aging, prophecy

**Nature:** Tho-Rienne was the reason that yesterday is not today, that actions have consequences, that you can remember your childhood. Without Tho-Rienne, all moments would exist simultaneously.

**Sacrifice:** Became the temporal Ley Lines — invisible threads that ensure time flows forward. The Timeless Wastes exist where Tho-Rienne was wounded and time became unreliable.

**Remnant Magic:**
- Chronomancy (forbidden time magic) draws on Tho-Rienne's weakened threads
- Prophecy is possible because Tho-Rienne's essence contains all moments
- Memory crystals (items that record events) are natural formations around temporal Ley Lines

**Modern Echo:** Déjà vu is a moment when Tho-Rienne's threads tangle briefly. The feeling that "time heals all wounds" is literally true — Tho-Rienne's flow carries pain downstream.

**Warning:** Tho-Rienne is the most damaged of the Seven. The Void Serpent targeted Time specifically, knowing that without causality, reality cannot maintain coherence. This is why time magic is so dangerous and why temporal paradoxes cause physical damage to the world.

---

### 4. Vel-Nara — The Principle of Space

**Domain:** Distance, dimension, direction, containment, vastness, connection

**Nature:** Vel-Nara was the reason that here is not there, that the world has size, that you can walk from one place to another. Without Vel-Nara, all things would occupy the same point.

**Sacrifice:** Became the spatial Ley Lines — the framework of distance itself. Dimensional travel is possible only through cracks in Vel-Nara's framework.

**Remnant Magic:**
- Teleportation magic exploits thin points in Vel-Nara's spatial web
- Dimensional pockets (bags of holding, etc.) are tears in Vel-Nara's fabric patched with magic
- The Spirit Membrane — the barrier between mortal and spirit world — is Vel-Nara's final gift

**Modern Echo:** The awe you feel standing on a mountaintop, looking at the vastness of the world — that's Vel-Nara. The vertigo of looking into a bottomless pit — that's Vel-Nara wounded.

---

### 5. Dro-Mahk — The Principle of Matter

**Domain:** Substance, weight, material, density, physical form, creation

**Nature:** Dro-Mahk was the reason that rock is hard, water is wet, and fire is hot. The physical properties of all things. Without Dro-Mahk, reality would be immaterial — thought without substance.

**Sacrifice:** Became the material Ley Lines — the deepest veins of magical metal ore that run through the planet's core. Mining too deep risks striking Dro-Mahk's veins and releasing wild material magic.

**Remnant Magic:**
- All crafting magic (enchanting, smithing, alchemy) channels Dro-Mahk's residue
- Golem creation is a crude imitation of Dro-Mahk's ability to give substance to purpose
- Adamantine and mithril are crystallized drops of Dro-Mahk's essence

**Modern Echo:** The satisfaction of holding something well-made — a perfect blade, a smooth stone, a warm loaf of bread. Dro-Mahk's love of substance echoes in the pleasure of physical things.

---

### 6. Ira-Sethi — The Principle of Energy

**Domain:** Motion, heat, light, lightning, life force, passion, destruction, creation

**Nature:** Ira-Sethi was the reason that things *happen*. The spark that makes hearts beat, lightning strike, fires burn, stars shine. Without Ira-Sethi, the universe would be cold, dark, and still.

**Sacrifice:** Became the energetic Ley Lines — the currents of raw magical energy that surge through the world. Magical storms occur when Ira-Sethi's lines discharge excess energy.

**Remnant Magic:**
- All elemental magic (fire, lightning, ice via heat removal) channels Ira-Sethi
- Healing magic works by channeling life energy — Ira-Sethi's most precious gift
- The Ashlands exist because Ira-Sethi burned too brightly during the Primordial War and the ground still remembers

**Modern Echo:** The surge of adrenaline in danger. The warmth of a hearth. The lightning in a storm. All of these are Ira-Sethi's heartbeat, still pulsing through the world.

---

### 7. Pho-Lumen — The Principle of Spirit

**Domain:** Consciousness, awareness, emotion, soul, meaning, identity, dreams

**Nature:** Pho-Lumen was the reason that anything in the universe can ask "why am I here?" The difference between a rock and a person. Without Pho-Lumen, the universe would exist but nothing would know it.

**Sacrifice:** Became the spiritual Ley Lines — the threads that connect every soul to every other soul, and to the world. The Spirit Membrane (barrier between living and dead) is Pho-Lumen's skin.

**Remnant Magic:**
- All soul magic, spirit magic, and psychic powers channel Pho-Lumen
- Dreams occur when sleeping minds drift along Pho-Lumen's spiritual threads
- The spirit world is Pho-Lumen's domain, maintained by sheer will even in death
- Necromancy is a perversion of Pho-Lumen's gift — forcing spirit back into matter

**Modern Echo:** The certainty that other people have inner lives. The ability to love. The terror of death. The wonder at a sunset. All of consciousness is Pho-Lumen's echo, the last gift of the last Original God.

## The Empty Thrones

When the Seven poured themselves into the world, their **divine thrones** remained. Each throne is not a physical chair but a *nexus of authority* — a point where all Ley Lines of a particular type converge. Sitting on a throne means becoming the conscious will that directs that type of energy.

The thrones exist in **The Threshold** — a place between places, accessible only to those who carry a "divine resonance" — a rare spiritual frequency that occurs naturally in roughly one in ten million mortals.

The thrones were designed to be empty. The Original Seven never intended anyone to sit in them. They sacrificed themselves so that the world could run on autopilot — Ley Lines flowing, magic ebbing and flowing with the tides, the Void Serpent caged by the pattern itself.

But empty thrones are an invitation. And seven of the worst people in history accepted it.

## Can the Original Seven Return?

The Restoration faction believes so. Theoretically, if someone could:

1. Find all seven Ley Line convergence points (each hidden in the most extreme environment of its type)
2. Channel enough energy to separate the Original Seven's essence from the world's fabric
3. Provide new vessels for their consciousness
4. Do all of this without the Ascended gods noticing and intervening

...then the Original Seven could be reconstituted. But this would mean:
- Temporarily destabilizing all magic worldwide
- Weakening the cage around Xal-Nepheth
- Directly challenging the Ascended, who would fight to keep their stolen thrones
- Risking the death of the world itself if the process fails

This is the ultimate endgame quest — the player must decide whether the truth is worth the risk.

# The Lore of Aethermoor

## Overview

This folder contains the deep lore of the game world — the hidden history that lies beneath the surface of what players and NPCs commonly believe. The world of **Aethermoor** is built on layers of truth and deception, where the gods themselves are the greatest liars of all.

## The Central Secret

The eight gods worshipped across the world — Solaris, Noctis, Ferrum, Verdana, Mortis, Fortuna, Lexis, and Mercatus — are merely **masks** worn by the true divine beings: **The Ascended**. These are not noble beings who earned their divinity. They are seven mortals who were the *worst* of humanity — criminals, cowards, tyrants, and monsters — who stole divine power from the dying Original Gods and were twisted by cosmic irony into embodiments of the very virtues they lacked.

Every temple hymn is a lie. Every holy scripture hides a crime. Every prayer reaches ears that once belonged to murderers.

## Connection to Existing Game Systems

The existing eight deities (Solaris, Noctis, etc.) are the **public-facing aspects** the Ascended project to mortals. The four religions (Church of the Radiant Sun, Cult of the Void, The Old Ways, The Clockwork Creed) are mortal interpretations of incomplete truths. The forbidden magic schools (blood magic, necromancy, soul magic) are echoes of the Original Seven's power that the Ascended failed to fully corrupt.

## Discoverability

Players uncover this hidden lore through:
- Ancient texts in forgotten languages (especially Primordial and Celestial)
- Spirit world encounters with pre-Ascension ghosts
- Racial questlines that brush against the truth
- The Unmaskers faction questline
- Legendary artifacts that predate the Ascended
- Forbidden library collections
- Dreams during rest in ancient ruins
- NPC scholars who have pieced together fragments
